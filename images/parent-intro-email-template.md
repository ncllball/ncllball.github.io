## PARENT EXPECTATIONS

This is a great group and I don't anticipate any issues — but it helps to say these things out loud at the start of every season:

- **No verbal abuse** of umpires, opposing coaches, or opposing players — ever
- **Stay positive in the stands** — cheer for your player and the team
- **Leave the coaching to the coaches** during games — your player needs one voice on the field
- **Communicate early** — if there's a schedule conflict, injury, or anything we should know about your player, please tell us in advance so we can plan

Our north star this season is **player development and enjoyment**. We also want to compete and win. Those goals are not in conflict. We will work hard, hold each other accountable, and have a lot of fun doing it.

If your player wants to try a specific position or has something on their mind, encourage them to come talk to me directly. This is a good age to practice speaking up for themselves.
