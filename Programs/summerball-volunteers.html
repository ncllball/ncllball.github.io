We Need Volunteers!

We are still looking for parent volunteers to help with coaching and managing teams. We know vacations will happen — even if you can only help with a few games, every bit counts.

Ways to help

Lead Game Volunteer
  • Bring team gear, help with setup, ensure safety
  • Handle unusual situations on game day
  • Especially needed in Majors and Teen Baseball
  • Advanced players may coach bases (helmet required)
Game Day Helper
  • Dugout manager, base coach, umpire, or parent pitcher (AA)
  • Especially needed for AA/AAA Baseball and Softball Sandlot
  • AAA players may coach bases (with supervision)
Team Manager
  • Organize RSVPs, coordinate snacks, communicate with families
  • Great for non-coaching volunteers — no game experience needed
Sandlot Team Organizer (A Baseball)
  • Organize players, balance teams, keep it fun, oversee safety
  • Ideal for NCLL's flexible "show up and play" format

Sign up as a volunteer and let your team coordinator know when you arrive at your first game.
