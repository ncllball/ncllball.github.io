We need your help to make Summerball26 a success

Summerball runs on volunteers. Whether you prefer being on the field or helping with coordination, there's a role for you.

Ways to help

  • Lead Game Volunteer (Head Coach): Bring team gear, help with setup, ensure safety, and handle unusual situations. Especially needed in Majors and Teen Baseball. Advanced players may coach bases (helmet required).
  • Game Day Helper (Assistant Coach): Provide on-field support — dugout manager, base coach, umpire, or parent pitcher (AA). Especially needed for AA/AAA Baseball and Softball Sandlot. AAA players may coach bases (with supervision).
  • Team Manager (Off-field logistics): Organize RSVPs, coordinate snacks, and help communicate with families. Great for non-coaching volunteers.
  • Sandlot Team Organizer (A Baseball Sandlot): Partner with other parents to organize players, balance teams, keep it fun, and oversee safety. Ideal for NCLL's flexible "show up and play" sandlot format.

Apply

You can indicate your interest when registering for Summerball26 and select from the roles above. Thank you for supporting our players and community!
