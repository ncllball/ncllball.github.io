## Background

Washington District 8 Challenger Division was started in **1991 by Gary and Bev Newsome** to give children with intellectual and physical disabilities a chance to play ball.

Today the program includes two leagues:

- **Challenger** — ages 4–18
- **Senior Challenger** — ages 15 and above

Combined enrollment: **100+ players** throughout the greater Seattle area. The program is supported by the 12 Little Leagues making up Washington District 8, as well as the **Seattle Mariners**, who continue to create special memories for these players.

[Learn more at Little League International](https://www.littleleague.org/play-little-league/challenger/)

## Registration

Registration is open! Register with your home Little League and play with teammates from across the District.

**Registration fee: $25**

Questions? Contact your home Little League or reach out to District 8 directly.
