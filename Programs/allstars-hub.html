Each year, North Central Little League (NCLL) sponsors several teams to compete in the Little League District 8 Tournament. The All-Stars teams represent our league and may have the opportunity to advance to the state tournament.

Let this space be both an introduction to those players and teams and a place to celebrate their accomplishments. This landing page features each year's All-Stars teams, organized by age group (10's, 11's, 12's, Intermediate, Juniors, and Seniors) and then by sport (Baseball followed by Softball). Click on any image to view it in full size.

To learn about the 2026 All-Stars teams, tryout dates, the application process, and more, visit the related links above.
