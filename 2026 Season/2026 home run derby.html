Save the Date! NCLL 2026 Home Run Derby – Wed 5/27

Wear your NCLL gear and bring a mitt! Come down and join your NCLL community and family at Lower Woodland, a.k.a. the Cloverleaf, for the annual North Central Little League Home Run Derby.

The event runs from 5:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m., and this year the Dick's Drive-In food truck will be there! Grab your favorite burgers while watching the derby and cheering on games on the other fields.

Bring your family, players, and an appetite—or even host your team party right at the field! Encourage your kids to wear their NCLL jerseys and bring a mitt to shag balls that cross the home run fence.

Event Schedule

  • 5:30 p.m.: Gates open
  • 6:00 p.m.: Softball Home Run Derby with Softball Majors players
  • 7:00 p.m.: Baseball Home Run Derby with Baseball Majors players
  • 8:30 p.m.: Event concludes
