Pre-pay for Photos

You should have received envelopes from the photographer with your child's uniform, but if not don't worry — you can PREPAY ONLINE by following the directions below.

How to Pre-pay Online

  1. Go to www.4nsp.com and select PREPAY ONLINE
  2. Parents can prepay as a guest or register for an account
  3. When asked if picture day has passed, choose "Continue to Prepay" unless picture day has already occurred
  4. Enter the Access Code and your State. The Access Code is R2526-01
  5. When your order is complete, print your confirmation page and bring it to the photo shoot — or write down the confirmation number on your NSP order envelope. The photographer will need your child's name and confirmation number at the time of photos.

What to Enter When Pre-paying

  1. Player First Name
  2. Player Last Name
  3. League — North Central Little League
  4. Team Name & Sponsor — please include the full sponsor name
  5. Sport — Baseball or Softball
  6. Coach's Name
  7. Email Address
