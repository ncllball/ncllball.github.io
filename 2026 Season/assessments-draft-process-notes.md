## Draft Day

There are actually two drafts within one. The top of the board is for returning AAA kids; the bottom is for new-to-AAA kids.
https://ncllball.github.io/images/draft/01.jpg

- Finish drafting as a group for all returning players, then start again on the bottom for new kids.
- If a coach has a kid in the division, they're told beforehand which round that kid will be taken — the coach is skipped in that round. Same as keepers in fantasy football.
- Coaches can bring assistants into the draft (confirm the current limit each year).

You sit in a room and keep going until everyone is picked. Any questions are fair game: Who knows this kid? What's his attitude like? Are his parents decent to deal with?

At the end there's a trade period. Not based on talent — more social engineering or sponsorships. If two kids are both a 2.5 but each has no one from their school on their team, you can swap them. Or if a coach wants their kid's best friend (who's a 1) on their team, they can trade another 1 for that kid.
