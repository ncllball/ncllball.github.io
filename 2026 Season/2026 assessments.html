2026 Player Assessments: What You Need to Know

We are finalizing exact windows with our facility partners. Expect confirmed schedules via email in early January along with SignUpGenius links.

Purpose & Objectives

Player assessments help us evaluate skills to ensure fair team placement and create balanced teams for a fun and competitive season. We will be assessing the following skills:

  • Running
  • Fielding
  • Throwing
  • Batting
  • Pitching (optional)
  • Catching (optional)

Mandatory Assessments

Assessments are mandatory for players seeking placement in these divisions:

  • Baseball AAA
  • Baseball Majors
  • Baseball Juniors
  • Softball AAA
  • Softball Majors

Assessment Windows & Locations

  • Baseball AAA/Majors: Late January 2026 (Saturday) | Target window 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM at Robert Eagle Staff Middle School
  • Softball AAA/Majors: Early February 2026 (Saturday) | Afternoon blocks at Wallingford Boys & Girls Club

Heads up: Exact check-in groups and times will be emailed and posted here once the gyms confirm final availability.

Registration & Volunteering

Players must be registered for the 2026 regular season before claiming a slot. We open assessment sign-ups in early January and will link them here.

  • Baseball AAA/Majors: Assessment registration (link coming early January) | Volunteer shifts will open alongside the player sign-up.
  • Softball AAA/Majors: Assessment registration (link coming soon) | To volunteer, please email Gayle Perez, VP of Softball while we finalize the SignUpGenius.

NOTE: If a link is marked “coming soon,” it simply means the facility schedule is still being confirmed. Watch your inbox for the launch email.
