You should have received envelopes from the photographer with your child's uniform, but if not don't worry as you can PREPAY ONLINE by following the below directions.

  1. Go to www.4nsp.com; go to PREPAY ONLINE
  2. Parents can prepay as a guest or register for an account.
  3. They will be asked if picture day has passed as this option is available through midnight of picture day. They should choose “Continue to Prepay” unless picture day has passed.  
  4. They will be asked for an Access Code and their State. The Access Code is R2425-01 
  5. When their order is complete, they will get a confirmation page. It is important that they print and bring this confirmation page to the photo shoot! Or write down the confirmation number on their NSP order envelope in the box next to the total. The Photographer will need to document their child’s image roll and frame number on the confirmation page or envelope with the player’s name and confirmation number at the time of photos.
