Each year, North Central Little League (NCLL) sponsors several teams to compete in the Little League District 8 Tournament. The All-Stars teams represent our league and may have the opportunity to advance to the state tournament.

Eligible Age Groups

  • 9/10-year-olds (AAA)
  • 10/11-year-olds (Majors/AAA)
  • 11/12-year-olds (Majors)

Being selected for an NCLL All-Stars team is a great honor and an exciting opportunity for players who want to compete at a higher level. All interested players are encouraged to apply!

2024 All-Stars Timeline

  • May 8 – Distribute All-Stars Player Applications to all league players
  • May 8 – Distribute Manager/Coach "Opt-In" Applications
  • May 23 – Deadline for submitting Player & Manager/Coach Applications
  • May 30 – All-Stars Managers selected (Managers will then select Coaches)
  • June 1-3 – Ballots distributed & collected from Players, Managers, and Coaches
  • June 4 – All-Stars Workouts
  • June 4-5 – Team selection meetings
  • June 6-7 – Selected players contacted
  • June 8 – All-Stars teams announced! Players no longer in NCLL playoffs can begin practicing
  • June 10 – NCLL Championship Games & Year-End Picnic

How to Apply

All players who wish to be considered for an NCLL All-Stars team must opt in by completing the official NCLL All-Stars Player Application.

  • Deadline to Apply: Sunday, May 21st – Turn in your completed application to your team's coach.
  • Email Option: If preferred, scan and email the application to registrar@ncllball.com by Tuesday, May 23rd at 9:00 PM.

If you need extra time to submit your application, please email us as soon as possible with an estimated submission date. This helps us plan for the selection process.
