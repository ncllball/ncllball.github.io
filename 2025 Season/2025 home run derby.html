NCLL Home Run Derby: A Community Celebration of Softball and Baseball!

Get ready for a highlight of the season! North Central Little League is thrilled to announce our annual Home Run Derby, taking place in just over a week. Mark your calendars and join us for an evening of thrilling competition, community spirit, and family fun at Lower Woodland Park.

This beloved event celebrates the hard work and dedication of our Majors Softball and Baseball players as they showcase their power-hitting skills on Field #6. We invite all NCLL families, friends, and supporters to come out and cheer on these talented athletes.

Make an evening of it! Bring a picnic to enjoy in the cloverleaf area or plan your team party right at the field. To make the event even sweeter, we'll be providing delicious Seattle Pops for all NCLL players and their siblings to enjoy while they take in the action.
