Home Run Derby: A Community Celebration

Get ready for one of the most exciting events of the season! North Central Little League is thrilled to announce our annual Home Run Derby on Wednesday, May 21, 2025. Come join us for a fun-filled evening of thrilling competition, community spirit, and family fun at Lower Woodland Park (LW#6).

The Main Event

This beloved event celebrates the hard work and dedication of our Majors Softball and Baseball players (11-12 year-old players) as they showcase their power-hitting skills on a modified Field #6. We invite all NCLL families, friends, and supporters to come out and cheer on these talented athletes.

Make a night of it! Bring a picnic to enjoy in the grassy cloverleaf area or plan a team party right at the field. To make the evening even sweeter, we'll be providing delicious Seattle Pops for all NCLL players and their siblings to enjoy.
