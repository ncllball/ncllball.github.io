2025 Player Assessments: What You Need to Know

Purpose & Objectives

Player assessments help us evaluate skills to ensure fair team placement and create balanced teams for a fun and competitive season. We will be assessing the following skills:

  • Running
  • Fielding
  • Throwing
  • Batting
  • Pitching (optional)
  • Catching (optional)

Mandatory Assessments

Assessments are mandatory for players seeking placement in these divisions:

  • Baseball AAA
  • Baseball Majors
  • Baseball Juniors
  • Softball AAA
  • Softball Majors

Assessment Dates & Locations

  • Baseball AAA/Majors: Saturday, January 25, 2025 | 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM at Robert Eaglestaff Middle School
  • Softball AAA/Majors: Saturday, February 8, 2025 | 12:15 PM - 6:00 PM at Wallingford Boys and Girls Club
