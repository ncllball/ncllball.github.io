Assessment Purpose: Player Evaluation for Team Placement

Assessment Objectives

Assessments assess players' abilities in key skill areas, including:

  • Running
  • Fielding
  • Throwing
  • Batting
  • Pitching (optional)
  • Catching (optional)

Assessment Purpose

These evaluations serve two primary purposes:

  • Divisional Placement — Assessments aid in determining the appropriate division for each player. By matching players with divisions that align with their skill levels, we foster fair competition.
  • Team Balancing — Assessments contribute to creating balanced teams. This balance enhances the playing experience for all individuals and supports a competitive and enjoyable season.

Assessment Requirements

Assessments are mandatory for players seeking placement in the following divisions:

  • Baseball AAA
  • Baseball Majors
  • Baseball Juniors
  • Softball AAA
  • Softball Majors
