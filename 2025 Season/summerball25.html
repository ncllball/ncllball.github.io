Why Summerball25?

1 - flexible schedule = no guilt!
2 - no practices, just games
2 - teams are sorted by friends
2 - ANYONE CAN PLAY! (No geographic/school boundaries)
3 - low-pressure, fun baseball
4 - great way to keep your player engaged in the game
5 - affordable at just $75 for the season

Register by: June 30
Season: We’ll start just after the 4th of July and run through the first or second week of August (10-12 games.)
Details: See Below for different program details!

Sign up today, grab your glove, and get ready to hit the field!

Single-A Baseball Summerball25

We are excited to offer our single-A families the chance to get some baseball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot style*, meaning maximum flexibility for families over the summer.
  • Players will have been in single-A or Coach Pitch already — NOT directly from T-Ball.
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or Lower Woodland.
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!

Please reach out to me with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

">registrar@ncllball.com

*Sandlot style means the kids show up and we pick teams based on who is there and ready to play ball. This way, there is no commitment to one team and families can come and go

**Games will be held on Mondays (2 Monday games), Tuesdays (3), Wednesdays (1), and Thursdays (4) for a total of 10 games.
