Why Summerball25?

  • Flexible schedule = no guilt! (This goes for coaches too!)
  • No practices, just games
  • Teams are sorted by friends
  • ANYONE CAN PLAY! (No geographic/school boundaries)
  • Low-pressure, fun baseball
  • Great & AFFORDABLE way to keep your player engaged in the game

Registration Dates: June 3rd - June 30th
Season Dates: Week after 4th of July through early/mid August (10-12 games.)
Program Cost: $75
Program Details: See Below for different program details!

Single-A Baseball Summerball25 (Sandlot Style)

We are excited to offer our single-A families the chance to get some baseball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot style*, meaning maximum flexibility for families over the summer.
  • Players will have been in single-A or Coach Pitch already — NOT directly from T-Ball.
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or Lower Woodland.
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!

Please reach out to me with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

*Sandlot style means the kids show up and we pick teams based on who is there and ready to play ball. This way, there is no commitment to one team and families can come and go

**Games will be held on Mondays (2 Monday games), Tuesdays (3), Wednesdays (1), and Thursdays (4) for a total of 10 games.

AA/AAA/MAJORS/JUNIORS Summerball25 (Intra)

We are excited to offer our single-A families the chance to get some baseball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot style*, meaning maximum flexibility for families over the summer.
  • Players will have been in single-A or Coach Pitch already — NOT directly from T-Ball.
  • The season will go 5 weeks from July 10th through August 11th with 2 games per week.
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or Lower Woodland.
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!
  • In the summer, we encourage kids to try out the level of baseball that they will play next year. So 12 year olds who played majors this year would play juniors summer ball on the larger field. Kids who are still developing their baseball skills can remain in the same level as the spring if it makes sense.
