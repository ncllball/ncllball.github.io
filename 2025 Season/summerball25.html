Why Summerball25?

  • Flexible schedule = no guilt! (This goes for coaches too!)
  • No practices, just games!
  • Teams are sorted by friends.
  • No geographic/school boundaries — ANYONE CAN PLAY!
  • Low-pressure, FUN baseball & softball!
  • Great & AFFORDABLE way to keep your player engaged in the game!

All Divisions

  • Registration Window: June 5th - June 30th
  • Program Dates: July 7th - August 14th
  • Format: Games only; no scheduled practices
  • Game Schedule: 2 games per week, total 10-12 games
  • Registration Fee: $75 per player
  • Uniform: Program jersey provided; wear spring season cap
  • Required Equipment: Baseball glove, protective cup (boys), footwear
  • Optional Equipment: Personal bat and helmet (shared equipment available)

Single-A Baseball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer families the chance to play Single-A baseball this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot1 format, giving maximum flexibility to players and coaches over the summer
  • For players with experience in Coach Pitch or Single-A only (not directly from T-ball)
  • Parent volunteers needed — all experience levels welcome! (See Side Bar)
  • See Matrix Below for more details on day, time, and locations

Please reach out with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1 Sandlot format means kids show up and teams are picked based on who is there and ready to play ball. This creates a flexible drop-in format with no commitment to one team, allowing families to come and go as their schedule allows.

Softball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer our softball families the chance to play ball this summer! For 2025, Softball will have a two-tiered program accommodating different skill and age levels. Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot1 format, bringing maximum flexibility to players and coaches over the summer
  • AA/AAA Softball is for developing AAA players and AA graduates
  • AAA/Majors Softball is for experienced AAA players and returning Majors players
  • Potential interleague play opportunities with neighboring leagues
  • Volunteer coaches needed — all are welcome to help! (See Side Bar)
  • See Matrix Below for more details on day, time, and locations

Please reach out with questions, including which division is right for your player: softball@ncllball.com

1 Sandlot format means kids show up and teams are picked based on who is there and ready to play ball. This creates a flexible drop-in format with no commitment to one team, allowing families to come and go as their schedule allows.

AA/AAA/MAJORS/JUNIORS Baseball Summerball25 (Interleague)

We are excited to offer our families in AA/AAA/Majors/Juniors a great chance to get some baseball in at Summerball25! Here are the details:

Organized baseball program with set teams and regular game schedules.1

  • Games will be interleague2 play, meaning competitively based against neighboring little leagues
  • Players are encouraged to play up to the level they'll play next year3
  • AA Baseball may have a hybrid mix of sandlot and interleague
  • Volunteer coaches needed — all are welcome to help! (See Side Bar)
  • See Matrix Below for more details on day, time, and locations

Please reach out with questions: paulfox11@gmail.com

2 Interleague refers to games played against teams from neighboring little leagues such as Ballard, Magnolia, North Central, Northeast, Northwest, Queen Anne, RUG, Shoreline, and North-End (NBLL).
