Why Summerball25?

  • Flexible schedule = no guilt! (This goes for coaches too!)
  • No practices, just games!
  • Teams are sorted by friends
  • No geographic/school boundaries -- ANYONE CAN PLAY!
  • Low-pressure, FUN baseball!
  • Great & AFFORDABLE way to keep your player engaged in the game!

All Divisions

  • Registration Window: June 3rd - June 30th
  • Program Dates: July 9th - August 14th
  • Format: Games only - no scheduled practices
  • Game Schedule: 2 games per week, total 10-12 games
  • Registration Fee: $75 per player
  • Uniform: Program jersey provided, wear spring season cap
  • Required Equipment: Baseball glove, protective cup (boys), footwear
  • Optional Equipment: Personal bat and helmet (shared equipment available)

Single-A Baseball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer families the chance to get some A-ball baseball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot1 format, giving maximum flexibility to players/coaches over the summer
  • For players with Coach Pitch or Single-A experience only (no direct from Tball)
  • Games at Ross Field and Lower Woodland Cloverleaf
  • Game times: 5:00 or 5:30 PM start
  • Typical game days2: Monday through Thursday (schedule varies)
  • Parent volunteers needed - all experience levels welcome!

Please reach out with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1 Sandlot format means kids show up and teams are picked based on who is there and ready to play ball. This creates a flexible drop-in format with no commitment to one team, allowing families to come and go as their schedule allows.

2 In 2024, Single-A saw: Mondays (2 games), Tuesdays (3), Wednesdays (1), and Thursdays (4) for a total of 10 games.

Softball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer families the chance to get some Softball in this summer! Here are the details:

Two-tiered program accommodating different skill and age levels.

  • Games will be sandlot1 format, giving maximum flexibility to players/coaches over the summer
  • AA/AAA Division: For developing players and AA graduates
  • AAA/Majors Division: For experienced players moving to Majors
  • Games at Ross Field and Lower Woodland
  • Game times: 5:00 or 5:30 PM start
  • Typical game days: Tuesdays and Thursdays
  • Interleague play opportunities with neighboring leagues
  • Volunteer coaches needed - all welcome to help!

Please reach out with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1 Sandlot style means the kids show up and we pick teams based on who is there and ready to play ball. This way, there is no commitment to one team and families can come and go.

2 Games refer to scheduled matches that follow the league's guidelines and structure, ensuring fair play and enjoyment for all participants.

AA/AAA/MAJORS/JUNIORS Summerball25 (Intraleague)

We are excited to offer our familes in AA/AAA/Majors/Juniors a great chance to get some baseball in at Summerball25! Here are the details:

Organized baseball program with set teams and regular game schedules.

  • Games will be Intraleague1 play, meaning competitively balanced against our local little league teams
  • Balanced team formation for competitive play
  • Players encouraged to play at next year's level
  • AA: Flexible schedule, hybrid format
  • AAA: Monday/Thursday games
  • Majors: Tuesday/Thursday games
  • Juniors: For graduating Majors players (12+)
  • Games at various local fields within 30-minute drive
  • Coaching volunteers needed at all levels
