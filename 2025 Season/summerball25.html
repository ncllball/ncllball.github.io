Why Summerball25?

  • Flexible schedule = no guilt! (This goes for coaches too!)
  • No practices, just games
  • Teams are sorted by friends
  • ANYONE CAN PLAY! (No geographic/school boundaries)
  • Low-pressure, fun baseball
  • Great & AFFORDABLE way to keep your player engaged in the game

All Divisions

  • Registration Dates: June 3rd - June 30th
  • Season Dates: July 9th - August 14th
  • Practices: There are NO practices
  • Games: There are two games per week; 10-12 games overall
  • Program Cost: $75
  • Uniforms: Each player receives a Summerball25 jersey; please wear your regular-season team cap.
  • Equipment: Bring a mitt, cup (for boys), and cleats or sneakers; helmets and bats provided, or bring your own.
  • Program Details: See below for specific information about each division.

Single-A Baseball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer families the chance to get some A-ball baseball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot1 style, meaning maximum flexibility for families over the summer
  • Players will have been in Single-A or Coach Pitch already — NOT directly from T-Ball
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or the Lower Woodland Cloverleaf.
  • Historically2, games have been on M/T/W/TH; however, days are not guaranteed and based on past schedules, not future
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!

Please reach out to me with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1 Sandlot style means the kids show up and we pick teams based on who is there and ready to play ball. This way, there is no commitment to one team and families can come and go.

2 In 2024, Single-A saw: Mondays (2 games), Tuesdays (3), Wednesdays (1), and Thursdays (4) for a total of 10 games.

Softball Summerball25 (Sandlot)

We are excited to offer families the chance to get some Softball in this summer! Here are the details:

  • Games will be sandlot style1, giving maximum flexibility to families over the summer
  • For 2025, Softball Sandlot is split into two groups for 2025; AA/AAA and AAA/Majors. If after reading this, you need help understanding where you belong, please reach out to softball@ncllball.com
  • AA/AAA is for experienced AA players and players playing AAA next year or players in the age range new to softball
  • AAA/Majors is for experienced AAA players planning to play Majors next year - we will likely co-league with neighboring leagues for this age group
  • Historically2, games have been on Tuesday's and Thursdays; however, days are not guaranteed and based on past schedules, not future
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or Lower Woodland.
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!

Please reach out to me with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1Sandlot style means the kids show up and we pick teams based on who is there and ready to play ball. This way, there is no commitment to one team and families can come and go.

2Games refer to scheduled matches that follow the league's guidelines and structure, ensuring fair play and enjoyment for all participants.

AA/AAA/MAJORS/JUNIORS Summerball25 (Intraleague)

We are excited to offer our familes in AA/AAA/Majors/Juniors a great chance to get some baseball in at Summerball25! Here are the details:

  • Games will be Intraleague1, meaning competitively balanced against our local little league teams
  • In the summer, we encourage kids to try out the level of baseball that they will play next year. So 12 year olds who played majors this year would play juniors summer ball on the larger field. Kids who are still developing their baseball skills can remain in the same level as the spring if it makes sense.
  • The season will go 5 weeks from July 10th through August 11th with 2 games per week.
  • Games will begin at either 5:00 or 5:30 at Ross Field or Lower Woodland.
  • MOST IMPORTANTLY — we need VOLUNTEERS to make this work so signup here and I will be reaching out. ANYONE can sign up to help regardless of coaching experience!!!

Please reach out to me with questions: registrar@ncllball.com

1Intra style refers to games played within the league, involving teams from neighborhoods such as Ballard, Magnolia, North Central, Northeast, Northwest, Queen Anne, RUG, Shoreline, and North-End (NBLL).

2Games refer to scheduled matches that follow the league's guidelines and structure, ensuring fair play and enjoyment for all participants.
