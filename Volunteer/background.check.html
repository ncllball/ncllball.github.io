All NCLL volunteers who interact with players must complete a background check each season. This helps ensure a safe environment for every participant and aligns with Little League policy and applicable law.

Do not forward background check results. NCLL is notified electronically when you complete the process.

At a Glance

Who needs it
Managers, assistant coaches, practice helpers, umpires, and volunteers interacting with players
When
Before your first practice or game
Renewal
Every calendar year
Provider
JDP (secure background screening)
Email link
Expires in 14 days; request a new one if it lapses
Help
See the Volunteer Hub or email treasurer@ncllball.com

How to Complete Your Background Check

  1. Register as a Volunteer

    • Go to the Volunteer Hub
    • Sign in and select your volunteer role

  2. Watch for the JDP Email

    • Look for an email invitation from JDP with a secure link
    • The link is valid for 14 days

  3. Complete the Questionnaire

    • Open the JDP link and fill out the required fields
    • Provide accurate, up-to-date information

  4. Submit and Wait for Confirmation

    • Submit the form; no additional upload is needed
    • NCLL is notified automatically when you’re cleared

If your JDP email link expires, contact treasurer@ncllball.com for a fresh invitation

Little League Policy

Little League may deny individuals the privilege of volunteering if a background check reveals information that may be detrimental to the safety or positive development of young people, other volunteers, and/or Little League International.
