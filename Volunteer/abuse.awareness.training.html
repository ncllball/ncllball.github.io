All NCLL volunteers must complete Little League's Abuse Awareness Training each season (≈ 20–25 minutes). The course helps you recognize and prevent abuse, support a safe environment, and understand reporting responsibilities. Completion is recorded automatically—no certificate upload required.

Need broader volunteer onboarding info? Visit the Volunteer Hub. A video walkthrough is also available.

All volunteers in contact with players (managers, assistant coaches, on‑field helpers, umpires, Board and key volunteers)

How to Complete the Training

  1. Access the Platform

    • Go to Little League University – Abuse Awareness Training
    • Create an account (first-time) or log in (returning)

  2. Enroll

    • Search for “Abuse Awareness Training” if not immediately visible
    • Open the course and click Enroll / Register

  3. Complete Modules

    • Work through each module’s video + reading segments
    • Answer any embedded questions or mini‑quizzes
    • (Optional) Take quick notes on key indicators & reporting steps

  4. Final Assessment

    • Finish the end-of-course quiz
    • Submit to record completion

  5. You're Done

    • System logs completion automatically
    • No code or certificate upload needed

No need to upload a certificate—NCLL is notified automatically after completion

Additional Tips

  • Technical issues: Use the platform Help / Support link
  • Deadline: Finish before your first scheduled practice or game
  • Returning volunteers: You must retake the course each calendar year
  • Reflect: Revisit key indicators periodically during the season

Questions?

Thank you for committing to a safe, positive environment for every player. If you need help, email registrar@ncllball.com.
