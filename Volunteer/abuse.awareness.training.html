Abuse awareness training is a mandatory course for all Little League volunteers, essential for ensuring the safety and well-being of all participants. This annual training equips volunteers with the knowledge to recognize and prevent abuse, create a safe environment, and understand their responsibilities. To complete the training, volunteers must access the training platform, create an account or log in, enroll in the course, complete all modules, pass a final assessment, and obtain a certificate of completion. Completion of abuse awareness training is a prerequisite for volunteering.

For more information, or to complete the registration process, visit our Volunteer Hub. For more direction, please see our Step-by-Step Guide for Abuse Awareness Training below.

Step-by-Step Guide for Abuse Awareness Training

We've also created a video walk-through to assist.

Step 1 — Access the Training Platform

  • Visit the Training Website: Go to the Abuse Awareness Training website at Little League International.
  • Create an Account: If you are a new user, click on "Sign Up" or "Register" to create an account. Enter your details including your full name, email address, and create a secure password.
  • Login: If you already have an account, click on "Login" and enter your email address and password.

Step 2 — Enroll in the Training Course

  • Find the Course: Once logged in, navigate to the "Courses" or "Training Programs" section of the website.
  • Search for the Course: Type "Abuse Awareness Training" in the search bar.
  • Enroll: Click on the course title to open the course page and then click on the "Enroll" or "Register" button.

Step 3 — Complete the Training Modules

  • Start the Course: Click on the "Start Course" or "Begin Training" button.
  • Follow the Modules: The training is divided into several modules. Complete each module by watching the videos, reading the materials, and taking any quizzes or assessments.
  • Take Notes: It's helpful to take notes during the training to ensure you understand the key concepts.

Step 4 — Take the Final Assessment

  • Complete the Assessment: At the end of the training, there will be a final assessment. Make sure to answer all the questions to the best of your ability.
  • Submit the Assessment: Once you have completed the assessment, click on the "Submit" button.

Step 5 — Obtain Your Certificate

  • You should now have your completion record. At one time we asked that you returned to the registration with a code/certificate, we no longer ask this. The league will be notified electronically when you complete the training.

Additional Tips:

  • Technical Support: If you encounter any technical issues, most training platforms have a "Help" or "Support" section. Don't hesitate to reach out for assistance.
  • Completion Deadline: Ensure you complete the training by your NEXT PRACTICE. This is important for compliance and maintaining the safety standards of our league.
  • Review and Reflect: Take some time to review the materials and reflect on the importance of abuse awareness in our community.

Thank you for your commitment to the safety and well-being of our players. Your dedication to abuse awareness training is essential for creating a safe and positive environment for all participants.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact the league administration at registrar@ncllball.com.
