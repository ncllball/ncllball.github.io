Schedule Coordinator

Are you organized? Can you meet deadlines and pay attention to details? Do you love Tetris and solving puzzles, getting all the pieces just so? Are you looking for a way to give back to the NCLL community?

Responsibilities

The Schedule Coordinator is responsible for scheduling fields for practices and games for the regular season. It is a critical and time-sensitive function ensuring all teams have field space to practice and play.

  • Coordinate field availability across all divisions for the full regular season
  • Meet scheduling deadlines to keep teams on track
  • Use software or data tools to manage and process scheduling information
  • Identify and apply process improvements where possible
