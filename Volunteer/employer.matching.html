Employer Matching Gifts

Did you know that many companies match not only their employees' monetary donations to nonprofits but also their volunteer hours with cash grants? These corporate philanthropy programs—often called Volunteer Match or Time Matching—can significantly benefit our league.

In Washington, a volunteer hour is valued at $27.54 (per Independent Sector). If a coach or team parent volunteers an average of 10 hours per week, that’s over $275 per week. Over a four-month season, that adds up to $4,400—funds your employer could be donating to North Central Little League (NCLL) just for the time you’re already giving!

Employer Giving & Volunteer Matching Programs

Did you know that many companies offer programs that allow employees to support non-profit organizations like North Central Little League (NCLL)? Companies such as Boeing, Starbucks, and Microsoft provide opportunities for employees to donate money or match volunteer hours with financial contributions.

Details on these programs are outlined below. NCLL is always looking for ways to connect with corporate partners, so if your employer has a similar program, please contact them for details and share this information with NCLL’s Sponsorship Director at sponsorships@ncllball.com.

Boeing Programs

Boeing Employees Community Fund (ECF) – Designated Giving Program

Boeing employees can donate a portion of their paycheck to NCLL through this payroll deduction program. North Central Little League qualifies as a recipient of ECF funds. More information is available on the Boeing ECF website.

Microsoft Programs

Microsoft GIVE Campaign

The GIVE Campaign is a gift matching program where Microsoft employees can donate money to qualifying nonprofits, with Microsoft matching those funds. NCLL is an eligible organization. Contact Microsoft for more details.

Microsoft Volunteer Connection System

Microsoft employees can log their volunteer hours spent supporting NCLL, and Microsoft will match their time with a cash gift. Contact Microsoft for more details.

Starbucks Matching Donations for Volunteer Time

Starbucks employees can match volunteer hours with financial contributions through the Partner Match program.

Follow these steps to submit your hours:

  1. Visit Starbucks Community Website
  2. Click Sign In as a Partner (log in with your global username/password)
  3. Click on My Projects, then select My Partner Match
  4. Search for North Central Little League using Organization ID 911065006 and type = Partner Time
  5. Select the correct listing for NCLL
  6. Enter your volunteer dates and hours

Does Your Employer Offer a Similar Program?
