Ways to Help

Sponsorships

Community sponsors keep NCLL strong - uniforms, fields, clinics, and scholarships all rely on local partners. We will match your business to the right division or event.

What you get

  • Logo on the front of a team uniform, on ncllball.com, and in league shout-outs.
  • End-of-season plaque to showcase your support.
  • Seasonal add-ons: banners, clinics, or event support.

Why it matters

  • Offsets costs for equipment, uniforms, field rentals, and maintenance.
  • Funds training materials and scholarships so every kid can play.
  • Builds community goodwill and connects your business to local families.

2025 season: $900 per team (covers uniforms). NCLL is a 501(c)(3).
