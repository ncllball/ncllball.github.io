Ways to Help

Sponsorships

Community sponsors keep NCLL strong - uniforms, fields, clinics, and scholarships all rely on local partners. We will match your business to the right division or event.

What you get

  • Logo on the front of a team uniform, on ncllball.com, and in league shout-outs.
  • End-of-season plaque to showcase your support.
  • Seasonal add-ons: banners, clinics, or event support.

Why it matters

  • Offsets costs for equipment, uniforms, field rentals, and maintenance.
  • Funds training materials and scholarships so every kid can play.
  • Builds community goodwill and connects your business to local families.

2025 season: $900 per team (covers uniforms). NCLL is a 501(c)(3).

Contact Sponsorships Full sponsor details

How to get started

  • Email your business name, contact, and preferred sport/division.
  • Send your logo (vector or high-res PNG) for uniforms and web.
  • Confirm team count: $900 per team covers full uniform set.
  • We pair you with a team and share key dates and payment details.

Good to know

  • Plaques delivered at season end to display at your business.
  • Recognition across site, social, events, and team gatherings.
  • Sponsorships help fund scholarships so every kid can play.

Registration Donations

Add a donation while you register your player. It is a fast way to fund scholarships and field upkeep alongside your regular fees.

How it works

  • During checkout, choose an optional donation amount that fits your budget.
  • Every dollar goes to scholarships, gear, and field maintenance.
  • Receipts come with your registration confirmation.

Volunteer Lite

Volunteer Lite is a lightweight way to pitch in with no long-term commitment or baseball experience required. Join an opt-in list and we will reach out when help is needed. If you are available, great; if not, maybe next time.

Ground Gremlins

Flash-mob field crew that swoops in after rain to keep diamonds playable.

  • Purpose: Rapid-response field work when weather hits
  • What you'll do: Rake, broom, sponge, lay turface, clear drains
  • Callouts: SMS-first for urgent needs; occasional email for weekend planning
  • Best for: Parents near our parks or anyone who can jump in on short notice
  • Frequency: Only when needed (rain days)
Join Ground Gremlins

Event Day Crew

Pop-up logistics squad that keeps Opening Day, Photo Day, and Derby shifts humming.

  • Purpose: Keep Opening Day, Photo Day, and Home Run Derby running smoothly
  • What you'll do: Set up, check-in, line management, breakdown
  • Callouts: Email-first with shift signups; occasional SMS reminders
  • Best for: Parents who like organized, time-boxed tasks
  • Frequency: A few events per season
Join Event Day Crew

Good to know

  • Anyone can volunteer: parents, grandparents, siblings, neighbors, community members.
  • Join or leave any list at any time.
  • Phone numbers are used only for urgent volunteer callouts.
  • To opt out: reply STOP to a text or email the organizer.
