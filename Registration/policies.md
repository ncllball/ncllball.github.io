# NCLL Registration Policies

## Refund Policy

The policy of North Central Little League is to issue no refunds after a child has played in a sanctioned game or practice. Prior to playing in a sanctioned game or practice, a refund of the registration fee less an administrative handling fee (equal to the registration late fee) will be issued.

Prior to sanctioned games or practices, if an out-of-boundary family is identified as having registered, they will be asked to leave the league unless no program is available to them in the league from the boundary where they live. A refund will be issued less $10.00.

## Privacy Policy

North Central Little League only collects personal information when you request our services, and only uses that information to provide those services. If you choose to make a purchase, you will be asked to provide contact and financial information for billing purposes only. All data is handled with the utmost confidentiality.

Personal information will not be traded, rented, sold, or otherwise shared with anyone outside of NCLL, or used for any reason beyond those contemplated in this statement. Third parties engaged to process information on our behalf have no permission to retain, share, store, or use personal information for any reason other than providing the requested service.

## Security

Your payment and personal information is protected by SSL encryption — the industry standard for secure commerce transactions. Your credit card number, expiration date, and CSV code are never stored in our systems. Our systems are routinely scanned by a third-party provider for security vulnerabilities in compliance with VISA/Mastercard policies.
