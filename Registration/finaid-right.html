NCLL ensures every child can play baseball/softball regardless of financial situation. No player is turned away for inability to pay. We offer full and partial scholarships through our confidential need-based assistance program.

To apply, complete the Financial Aid form before registering. Apply early to ensure processing before deadlines. Approved families receive a promotional code for registration to be entered into your cart. That will come via email from our Registrar.

Assistance covers registration fees, equipment (when available), and special programs. Questions? Contact our Registrar: registrar@ncllball.com
