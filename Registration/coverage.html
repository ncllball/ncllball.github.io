Thank you for your interest in Little League baseball! When you pay your fees, you’re supporting various essential aspects of our program. Here’s what those fees cover (consider this list representative, not exhaustive):

How Fees Are Distributed

  • Fields — $46,884 (24.92%)
  • Unis — $53,818 (28.59%)
  • Equipment — $16,907 (8.98%)
  • Umpire — $4,939 (2.62%)
  • Fall/Winter Dev — $4,384 (2.33%)
  • Coach/Player Dev — $8,055 (4.28%)
  • All‑Stars Unis — $21,866 (11.62%)
  • Summerball — $3,449 (1.83%)
  • Overhead — $27,925 (14.84%)

iBased on NCLL 2025 budget summary

Core Operating Costs: Overview

  • Charter & insurance: safety compliance required by Little League International.
  • Fields: permits, rentals, and on‑field materials/maintenance (chalk, bases, mound clay) to keep diamonds playable.
  • Equipment & uniforms: league‑supplied jerseys, hats/caps, socks (AAA+ also receive hoodies) and shared gear (catcher’s equipment, helmets, bats, tees, practice balls, first‑aid kits).
  • Umpires: training and per‑game fees where applicable.
  • Volunteer screening & safety: background checks and required certifications.
  • Player & coach development: clinics, assessment logistics, scheduling tools, and seasonal development (including fall/winter).
  • Community & events: opening day and other community‑building activities.
  • Summerball: support for off‑season programming.
  • All‑Stars uniforms: tournament uniform costs.
  • Operations & overhead: registration systems, communications, administration, and general expenses.
  • Scholarships: need‑based assistance so ability to pay is never a barrier.
