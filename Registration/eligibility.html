League Boundary Map & School Enrollment Eligibility

North Central Little League (NCLL) competes in Washington District 8 and serves families across Wallingford, Green Lake, Fremont, Phinney Ridge, Greenwood, and Ballard. Players qualify for NCLL by meeting one of the eligibility paths below.

This year's registration is more aggressive in asking for proof of eligibility, but please note: It is NOT REQUIRED by NCLL—this is a Little League International question and does not need to be answered in order for a successful registration. We will ask for this paperwork if your player makes All-Stars.

Residency

You only need one eligibility path—either residency or school enrollment.

  • Players who live inside the official NCLL boundary are eligible to register.
  • Not sure which league covers your address? Use the Little League International League Finder Tool to confirm your home league.

School Enrollment

You only need one eligibility path—either residency or school enrollment.

  • Players enrolled at schools located inside the NCLL boundary are also eligible, even if they live outside it.
  • If your school is missing from the list below, choose “Other” during registration and type the school name. This helps us capture boundary adjustments and confirm eligibility.
  • Preschools do not affect eligibility. T-ball registration is open regardless of school or residence—feel free to note your preschool for placement purposes only.
