Eligibility Requirements

North Central Little League (NCLL) is in District 8 and encompasses parts of Wallingford, Greenlake, Fremont, Phinney Ridge, Greenwood, and Ballard. To play for North Central Little League, you must meet ONE of the following criteria:

Residency

  • You must live within NCLL boundaries.
  • You can use the online League Finder Tool managed by Little League International to find your local Little League by address.

School Enrollment

  • Your player must attend a school within NCLL boundaries.
  • If your school is not listed below, and your address is considered out of boundary, you may select “Other” under Little League School Name and write in the school name in the space provided. This option is useful if you are impacted by boundary changes and seek eligibility to North Central Little League.
  • Preschools are not considered for eligibility; enrollment in tee ball is not subject to boundary requirements. Feel free to enter the preschool name in “Other School Name” for grouping purposes.
