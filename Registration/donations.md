Please consider contributing to the direct appeal — NCLL's primary fundraiser. By doing so, you support players, coaches, and our community.

The fees families pay and the sponsorships we receive from local businesses do not quite cover the costs of running the league. To bridge that gap and keep providing a high-quality youth sports program, we need your donations.

## Your donation supports:

- **Positive Coaching Alliance Partnership** — Training for coaches to help teach life lessons through baseball and softball. NCLL has partnered with PCA for five years and has been recognized by other leagues as a model for sportsmanship, respect, and teamwork.
- **Financial Aid** — No child is turned away from NCLL due to lack of funds. We provide financial support to every family that needs it.
- **Safety Equipment** — Continually updated protective gear for players and umpires.
- **All-Star Team Travel**
