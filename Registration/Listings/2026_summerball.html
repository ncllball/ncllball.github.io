NCLL Summerball is our annual summer baseball and softball league — affordable, fun, and perfect for players who want to keep playing without the pressure of the regular season.

What you get:

– $75 per player for the full season

– July 6 – August 14 — approximately 10–12 games

– Program jersey included

– No practices — just games, twice a week

– Open to all players regardless of home league

Registration closes June 30. Teams are announced around the July 4th weekend.

Questions? Contact registrar@ncllball.com or summerball@ncllball.com. More info at ncllball.com/summerball.
