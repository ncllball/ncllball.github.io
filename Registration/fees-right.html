2025 NCLL Registration Fees

Division fees are listed separately for Baseball and Softball. Important notes follow below.

The NCLL registration fee helps cover core operating costs required to run a safe, fun, volunteer-driven Little League program each season:

  • Little League International charter, insurance, safety compliance
  • Field use, permits, maintenance materials (chalk, bases, mound clay)
  • League-supplied uniforms (shirts / caps — plus AAA/Major hoodie upgrade, see note below)
  • Shared equipment (catcher’s gear, tees, practice balls, first aid kits)
  • Umpire training / game fees (where applicable)
  • Background checks & required safety training for volunteers
  • Player development (clinics, assessment logistics, scheduling tools)
  • Scholarship support so that ability to pay is never a barrier
