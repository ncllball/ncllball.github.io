# 2024 NCLL Home Run Derby & Ice Cream Social

## Event Details
- **Date:** Thursday, May 30, 2024, 5:30–8:00 p.m.
- **Location:** Lower Woodland Field 5
- **Ice Cream:** Ben & Jerry's (one free scoop for players, siblings, and adults in NCLL gear)
- **Notes:** Seattle Parks Department vacuumed up infield puddles after nonstop rain the prior Wednesday to make the event happen.
