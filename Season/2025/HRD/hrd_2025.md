# 2025 NCLL Home Run Derby

## Event Details
- **Date:** Wednesday, May 21, 2025, 5:30 p.m.
- **Location:** Lower Woodland Field 5
- **Treat:** Seattle Pops (for all NCLL players and siblings)
- **Notes:**

## Key Volunteers / Roles
- **Jonathan Pérez** — MC/announcer
- **Colin Becker** — home run umpire
- **Riko Hibino** (Lincoln HS junior) — threw first pitch. Her Change.org petition for turfed softball fields had 1,111+ signatures as of event day; tied to a Seattle Times front-page article "Seattle has a softball field problem" (May 16, 2025)
- **Carol Thompson** — board president
- **Gayle Pérez** — softball president, coordinated contestants

## Food
- Hot dogs (for all families)
- Ben & Jerry's ice cream — 1 free scoop for NCLL players and volunteers
- Fundraiser QR code on-site for All-Stars teams

## Notable Moments
- Nearly rained out — Seattle Parks applied turface and removed puddles from field 5 day-of
- Event streamed live on GameChanger (first time)
- Nora Dodgion's 54+ HRs dwarfed the field — Ruby was 2nd with just 9
- Baseball came down to a swing-off tiebreaker — Luke won with 17 total HRs
- Colin Becker served as home run umpire (his idea, coordinated with John Shikella)
- Professional student photographer attended — additional photos may exist
