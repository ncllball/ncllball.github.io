# 2026 NCLL Home Run Derby

## Event Details
- **Date:** Wednesday, May 27, 2026, 5:30 p.m.
- **Location:** Lower Woodland Field #6
- **Treat:** Seattle Pops (500 pops; pickup by Jon Way)
- **Notes:** First mother ever to pitch in the NCLL Baseball Home Run Derby (Emma York-Jones for son Elliot York-Jones).

## Baseball
**Winner:** Luke Jacobsen
**Contestants (back row L→R):** Auggie Hilen, Harlan Putman, Quinault Fuller, Will Millea, Alex Dallmeyer, Elliot York-Jones, Austin Christofferson, Luke Jacobsen, Henry Tufte
**Front row:** Brooks Bressler, Lucas Wickland
**Not pictured:** Kai Johnston
**Pitcher:** Emma York-Jones (mother of Elliot — historic first)

## Softball
**Winner:** Avery Baker
**Contestants (L→R):** Nora Dodgion, Elisa Pérez, Avery Baker, Ruby Way, Izzi Parsons
**Not pictured:** Noe Smith, Cami Richardson
**Pitcher(s):**
- **Notes:** Nora Dodgion completed her final year of Little League eligibility; had held the crown for 3 consecutive years (2023–2025). Title was wide open — Avery claimed it.
