# 2022 NCLL Jamboree & Funko Games Home Run Derby

## Event Details
- **Date:** Wednesday, June 8, 2022, 6:00–8:00 p.m.
- **Location:** Lower Woodland Fields 4 and 5
- **Title Sponsor:** Funko Games
- **Ice Cream:** Ben & Jerry's (free for NCLL kids; adults with NCLL gear get a scoop too)
- **Other Activities:** Tug-of-war
- **Food:** Ben & Jerry's ice cream (for players; adults in NCLL gear)
- **Other Activities:** Tug-of-war (multiple age groups: T-ball, A, AA)
- **Special Guest:** Mariners Moose (planned)
- **Notes:** Funko Games was title sponsor. Board discussed limiting tug-of-war to 1 age group due to capacity concerns (~1,620 people estimated if all teams attended). David Gonzalez led Home Run Derby coordination.
