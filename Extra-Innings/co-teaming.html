For Little League All-Star teams, "co-leaguing" (sometimes called "co-teaming" or "tournament combination") allows 2-3 leagues to combine their eligible players to form a single All-Star team for tournament play.

This is only approved by Little League International when individual leagues do not have enough eligible players to field a full All-Star team on their own, especially in divisions like Softball or the older Teenage Divisions. It requires a specific Tournament Combination Request Form and strict approval from District, Regional, and International Little League committees.
