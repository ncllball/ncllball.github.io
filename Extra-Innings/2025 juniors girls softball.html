The ladies now advance to the West Region tournament in Tucson, AZ!

The team's opportunity to compete for the chance to advance to the Junior Softball Little League World Series starts in Tucson, AZ, with games running from Friday, July 18th through Wednesday, July 23rd, 2025 (see link below for specific game data). The team, known as the 'Queens', consists of players from Championship and "if necessary" games to win the state title.

Visit the Tournament Website for schedule details, rules, venue information, and everything else you need to know about the West Region tournament. This official site provides all essential information for players and families.

Shoreline LL, North Central LL, and Queen Anne LL are 100% volunteer run non-profit organizations that rely on generous donations from the community to support budgets for travel to state, regional, and national tournament venues. Every donation, big or small, goes directly toward travel, lodging, and meal costs for our players and their families.
