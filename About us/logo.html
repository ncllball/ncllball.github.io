NCLL Logos & Brand Guide

Think of this page as a quick tour of the symbols your family sees around the fields. Each section explains what the mark represents, where players and parents will spot it, and includes an easy download if you ever need the artwork.

NCLL Badge

The badge links our league name to the heritage clover, so families immediately know they’re home. You’ll see it on welcome signs, registration emails, and the first slide of every presentation.

We lead with the badge whenever we introduce something new so parents, players, and fans get the full North Central impression right away.

Primary Clover Logo

The clover is the everyday emblem you’ll spot on hats, dugout banners, and team newsletters. Choose the color treatment that fits your background—white, black, or red—and it will look just like the gear the kids wear.

Give the clover a little breathing room and keep its shape intact so the mark feels as balanced on your family’s hoodies as it does on the scoreboard.

Secondary Wordmark

The wordmark simply spells out “North Central Little League,” which is helpful for new parents previewing sign-up sheets, sponsor flyers, or team store listings. Drop it anywhere you want the league name to be instantly readable.

Tertiary (Flat) Logo

This simplified logo is the go-to for embroidery, patches, and budget-friendly fundraising gear. It keeps the clover’s personality while printing cleanly on helmets, decals, and single-color spirit wear.

Typography

Prohibition is the bold lettering you see on uniforms and team banners—it keeps every age group feeling connected. Online we pair it with easy-to-read fonts (Cronos, Source Sans, etc.) so updates to parents stay clear on phones and tablets.

  • Prohibition: Primary display type for uniforms and large statements.
  • Cronos/Source Sans: Supporting fonts for long-form copy, email, and the website.
  • Always keep adequate letter spacing and avoid faux italics; instead, lean/italicize using official font files.

Rollout & What’s Next

You’ll keep seeing the refreshed look pop up on coach gear, volunteer jackets, and player hoodies. A new swag store is on the way too—every order sends 10% back into scholarships, development, and league projects the whole community benefits from.

Expect to see:

  • Updated signage and field branding throughout the season.
  • Digital channels (website, emails, social) refreshed with the badge and clover system.
  • Giveaways and contests on social media highlighting new merch drops.
