NCLL Logos & Brand Guide

Use the references below when you need an official North Central Little League mark. Each section links to downloadable artwork and explains how the assets should be used on uniforms, swag, signage, and league communications.

NCLL Badge

Our badge ties everything back to the league name and the heritage clover. Use this mark on web properties, email headers, social posts, and letterhead when you need a full-league identifier.

The badge should always be the first mark fans and families see when we introduce a new property.

Primary Clover Logo

The clover insignia is our everyday mark. It shows up on ball caps, field signage, digital channels, and day-to-day communications. Use the treatment that best fits the background—white, black, or red.

Keep adequate padding around the clover and avoid stretching or modifying the proportions.

Secondary Wordmark

Our horizontal wordmark pairs “North Central” with “Little League” in the new condensed typeface. Use it on apparel, banners, or situations where text clarity is essential (e.g., sponsor decks, signage).

Tertiary (Flat) Logo

The flat logo is designed for embroidery, single-color printing, and spots where gradients or strokes are difficult to reproduce. Think helmets, equipment decals, or budget-conscious merch runs.

Typography

Prohibition is our hero typeface. Use it for apparel and logo lockups to maintain a consistent personality. On the web we pair it with our core UI fonts (Cronos, Source Sans, etc.) for readability.

  • Prohibition: Primary display type for uniforms and large statements.
  • Cronos/Source Sans: Supporting fonts for long-form copy, email, and the website.
  • Always keep adequate letter spacing and avoid faux italics; instead, lean/italicize using official font files.

Rollout & What’s Next

The refreshed brand is already appearing on coaches’ gear, volunteer apparel, and player hoodies. We’re also launching a new swag store—10% of every purchase goes back into scholarships, player development, and league initiatives.

Expect to see:

  • Updated signage and field branding throughout the season.
  • Digital channels (website, emails, social) refreshed with the badge and clover system.
  • Giveaways and contests on social media highlighting new merch drops.
