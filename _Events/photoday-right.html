Photo Day 2026 is back — and once again, we'll be spreading it across multiple dates! Last year's multi-day format was a great success, giving families more flexibility and making the experience smooth for everyone.

We've thoughtfully organized the schedule to make it as convenient as possible, especially for families with players in multiple divisions.

Team photos are one of the best parts of the Little League experience — don't miss your chance to capture these memories!

Photos are taken at the Cloverleaf at Lower Woodland, near the batting cages (along the outer left field of LW Field #5). If you are only there for photos, the best parking (if available) is the parking lot by the tennis courts, as it is just down the hill. We look forward to seeing you!

Tips for Photo Day

  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early
  • Wear full uniform (jersey, pants, belt, socks, cap)
  • Bring your mitt; leave bats in the car unless instructed
