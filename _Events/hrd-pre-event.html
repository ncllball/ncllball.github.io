Save the Date! NCLL [YEAR] Home Run Derby – [DAY] [MONTH/DATE]

Wear your NCLL gear and bring a mitt! Come down and join your NCLL community and family at Lower Woodland, a.k.a. the Cloverleaf, for the annual North Central Little League Home Run Derby.

The event runs from 5:30 pm to 8:30 pm, and this year the Dick's Drive-In food truck will be on-site from 5:30 to 7:30 pm! Grab your favorite burgers while watching the derby and cheering on games on the other fields.

Bring your family, players, and an appetite — or even host your team party right at the field! Encourage your kids to wear their NCLL jerseys and bring a mitt to shag balls that cross the home run fence.

Event Schedule

  • 5:30 pm: Gates open; Dick's Drive-In food truck arrives
  • 6:00 pm: Softball Home Run Derby with Softball Majors players
  • 7:00 pm: Baseball Home Run Derby with Baseball Majors players
  • 7:30 pm: Dick's Drive-In food truck departs
  • 8:30 pm: Event concludes
