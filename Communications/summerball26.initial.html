Why Summerball26?

The Perfect Summer Program

  • Affordable — just $75 for the entire season
  • Convenient — TWO games per week (10–12 games total)
  • Relaxed — NO practices, just show up and play!
  • Flexible — ideal for families with summer travel plans
  • Fun-focused — low-pressure environment for all skill levels
  • All-inclusive — program jersey provided with registration
  • Open to all — no geographic boundaries; anyone can play!

Key Dates

  • Registration closes: June 30
  • Season: July 7 – ~August 13 (10–12 games)
  • Teams announced: ~July 4th weekend via email

We Need Your Help!

Parent volunteers make Summerball26 possible! Have summer vacation plans? No problem — even helping with a few games makes a huge difference. All experience levels are welcome. Sign up to volunteer during the same registration process as your player — we’ve got a role for everyone!
