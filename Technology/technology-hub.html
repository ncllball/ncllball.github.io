GameChanger Help

Set up streams, scoring, and roster access in GameChanger.

GameChanger hub

Download GameChanger and set your roster, share the team code, and enable live scoring.

How to join your team

  1. Install GC and create an account.
  2. Tap “Find Team” and search your team name or paste the invite link from your coach.
  3. Request to join as a family member; coaches approve in the app.
  4. Turn on notifications for game times, lineup, and score updates.

If you can’t find your team, confirm the exact team name and season with your coach. Keep notifications on for live scoring and chat.

Where to get help: see the GC hub for streaming, scoring shortcuts, and contacts.

“Team Central” Help

Official schedules, standings, and team pages in Team Central (Sports Connect).

Sports Connect hub
  • Team Central: schedules, standings, and team pages.
  • Registration is linked from our Register tab; keep account info current.

Team Central vs. GameChanger

Team Central is the league portal for official schedules and standings. GameChanger is the day-to-day app for live scoring, roster, chat, and notifications. Follow Team Central for official times; use GC for live updates.

If you hit login issues, update your account info or reset your password via Sports Connect.

Need more help? Open the Sports Connect hub for links and support.
