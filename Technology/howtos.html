Sports Connect

Change a user's email address

  1. Log in to your Sports Connect admin account.
  2. Navigate to the User Management section.
  3. Search for the individual by name.
  4. Open their profile and select the option to edit contact information.
  5. Replace the old email with the new one and save.

If you run into issues, Sports Connect offers live phone support — see sportsconnect.com/support.
