NCLL Google Chat Hub

We use a league-wide Google Chat space so parents and coaches can stay connected before and during the season. We’ll share the join link before opening day; use the guide below to join, set notifications, and follow the ground rules.

League Google Chat

Join the league-wide Google Chat space for updates, reminders, and quick Q&A with coaches and board members.

Request the Chat Link

Who should join

  • Parents/guardians for league-wide and division announcements.
  • Managers and coaches to coordinate quickly with families and board.
  • Board/key volunteers to share timely reminders (field updates, weather, events).

We do not add players to this space; keep player PII out of the chat.

How to join (desktop & mobile)

  1. Click the invite link (shared via email/Team Central) and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Accept the invite to the NCLL space; rename the space if you like.
  3. On mobile: install Google Chat (iOS/Android), tap the invite link, and choose your Google account.

Keep the link handy; families with multiple parents can each join the space.

Notifications

  • Desktop: gear icon → Settings → Notifications; choose “All” or “First @mentions” based on preference.
  • Mobile: tap the space name → Notifications → set to “All” during season, then dial back if noisy.
  • Mute threads that don’t apply to you; leave the space after the season if desired.

Ground rules & support

  • Be concise and respectful; no player PII or posting photos without consent.
  • Use @mentions sparingly; keep team-specific items in your team channel when possible.
  • Need the link or access help? Email registrar@ncllball.com.
