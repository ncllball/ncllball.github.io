Technology

Tools we use to run the league: GameChanger for live scoring and team chat, Sports Connect for registration and league websites, plus a few other helpers.

GameChanger

  • Download GameChanger
  • Parents: join your team in the app; ask your coach for the invite link
  • Coaches: set your roster, share the team code, and enable live scoring

How to join your team

  1. Install the GameChanger app and create an account
  2. Tap “Find Team” and search your team name, or paste the invite link from your coach
  3. Request to join as a family member; coaches approve requests in the app
  4. Turn on notifications to get game times, lineup, and score updates

Tip: If you can’t find your team, confirm the exact team name and season with your coach

Sports Connect

  • Team Central — schedules, standings, and team pages
  • Registration: linked from our Register tab on this site
  • Tip: keep your account info updated to avoid login issues

Other tools

  • Little League Age Calculator
  • Field locations & maps
  • Weather: follow Seattle Parks and school field updates for closures

FAQ

GameChanger vs. Team Central — what’s the difference?

GameChanger is your team’s day‑to‑day app: live scoring, chat, roster, and notifications. Team Central (Sports Connect) is the league portal: official schedules, standings, and team pages. Many teams use both — follow Team Central for the official schedule, use GameChanger for live updates and communication
