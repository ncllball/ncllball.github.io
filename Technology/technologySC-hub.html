SC Calendar Import

Subscribe to your Sports Connect team schedule so games and practices stay synced in Google, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.

Open full guide

Grab the Sports Connect link

  1. Sign in at ncllball.com > Team Central > Team Directory.
  2. Select your team, open the Calendar tab.
  3. Click Subscribe / Download to reveal the long .ics link.
  4. Copy the full link (Control/Command + C) to paste into your calendar app.

Subscribing keeps the calendar in sync when coaches move practices or add make-up games.

Add to Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar.
  2. Under Other calendars, click + > From URL.
  3. Paste the Sports Connect link and click Add calendar.
  4. Rename via the 3-dot menu > Settings.

Google refreshes subscribed calendars every few hours; urgent changes still come via coach emails/chats.

Add to Outlook

Outlook on the web
  1. Go to Outlook Calendar.
  2. Add calendar > Subscribe from web, paste the link, name it, and import.
Outlook desktop
  1. Calendar view > Add/Subscribe from Web (New) or Open Calendar > From Internet… (Classic).
  2. Paste the link and save.

If you imported a file instead of subscribing, delete it and subscribe to the URL so updates sync.

Add to Apple Calendar

On a Mac
  1. Calendar app > File > New Calendar Subscription….
  2. Paste the link, subscribe, pick color/refresh frequency.
On iPhone / iPad
  1. Copy the link.
  2. Settings > Calendar > Accounts > Add Account > Other > Add Subscribed Calendar.
  3. Paste into Server, tap Next, adjust options, tap Save.
Troubleshooting
  • Subscribe vs. import: make sure the link ends with .ics and you subscribed, not a one-time import.
  • Missing updates: wait for the next sync window or re-subscribe with the full link.
  • Multiple teams: subscribe each team separately; toggle visibility per calendar.
