Softball Overview

Below are NCLL's Softball Divisions. Please note that softball is designated for girls. If you believe your player is capable of playing at a higher division than initially offered, indicate this during the registration process in the space provided. This will be decided at asessments (AAA & Majors), so please sign up and plan on attending.

A Division - Coach Pitch

  • Age 6 & 7 (Girls age 6 should have at least one year of T-Ball experience)
  • 2 events per week — Tuesday and Saturday*
  • Practices will be 60-90 minutes until games begin
  • Primary focus is on skill and game development with fair playing time
  • Coach pitch
  • 10’’ safety ball
  • Pitching rubber is 35ft from home plate
  • No playoffs at this level

AA Division - Coach/Player Pitch

  • Age 7 to 9
  • 2 events per week — Wednesday and Saturday*
  • Practices will be 60-90 minutes until games begin
  • Primary focus is on skill and game development with fair playing time
  • Coach / Kid pitch
  • 11’’ softball
  • Pitching rubber is 35ft from home plate
  • No playoffs at this level

AAA Division - Player Pitch 

  • Age 9 to 11
  • 3 events per week — Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday*
  • Practices will be 60-90 minutes until games begin
  • Primary focus is on skill and game development with fair playing time
  • Player Pitch
  • 11’’ softball
  • Pitching rubber is 35ft from home plate
  • Playoffs will follow the regular season with more information to come
  • Asessments required

Majors Division

  • Age 10 to 12
  • 3 events per week — Monday, Wednesday, Friday OR Saturday*
  • Practices will be 60-90 minutes until games begin
  • Primary focus is on skill and game development with fair playing time
  • Player Pitch
  • 12’’ softball
  • Pitching rubber is 40ft from home plate
  • Playoffs will follow the regular season with more information to come
  • Asessments required

Juniors Division

  • Age 13 & 14
  • 3 events per week — Monday, Friday, Sunday*
  • Practices will be 60-90 minutes until games begin
  • Primary focus is on skill and game development with fair playing time
  • Player Pitch
  • 12’’ softball
  • Pitching rubber is 43ft from home plate
  • Playoffs will follow the regular season with more information to come
