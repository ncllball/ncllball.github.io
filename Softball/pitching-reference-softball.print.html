Rotation Principles

  • AAA/Majors: 2–3 pitchers weekly minimum.
  • Short stints early; lengthen mid-May.
  • Track cumulative weekly innings; flag overages.
  • Never 3 consecutive calendar days in circle.

Fatigue Signs

  • Arm wrap / recoil increases.
  • Stride shortens; upright finish.
  • Release timing late (high sail).
  • Spin axis inconsistent vs warm-up.

Dual / External Load

  • Log select / school innings separately.
  • At least 1 full rest day weekly (April/May).
  • Limit novelty pitches before Majors.
  • Recovery: hydration + light band work next day.
