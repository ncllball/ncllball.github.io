*Notes on Days of the Week

The scheduled days of softball events is listed under each division. This schedule was established long before our time and is agreed upon by all leagues in District 8. Although occasional tweaks are made, they are always in coordination with the district.

For instance, if Majors play on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday at the district level, we must schedule our Majors games on those days.

As a result, requests such as, "I would like to coach but can only make it on..." are nearly impossible to accommodate and largely out of our hands.

*Notes on Practice Days

Practice days will make every effort to match the days of the week games will be scheduled, however this is a goal, not a guarantee
