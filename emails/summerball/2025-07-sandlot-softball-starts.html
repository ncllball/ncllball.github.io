Welcome to Summer Ball! We will be teaming up with our neighbors in Shoreline & RUG this summer. Please see below for information about the summer ball format, days, and locations. NCLL will provide jerseys - we could use a volunteer to help distribute them.

We are excited to have your player join us for the RAPID SOFTBALL summer ball division. This division will be for players 6-9 years old and will follow Rapid Softball guidelines and format: www.rapidbaseball.org 

Summer Ball Season starts this Monday 7/7 at Paramount Park North! This group will meet every Mon and Wed evening from July 7th through August 6th from 6-8pm. We can always use more volunteers to run summer ball, if you are interested/able please jump in and help! 

Please be sure that your player is prepared with their glove, tennis shoes/cleats, a hat or visor, and water bottle. Optional for them to bring other items such as a bat/helmet/ etc. Equipment besides their glove will be provided/available for group use. A reminder that summer ball is session based, not team based, and we understand it is summer and that kids will likely miss some sessions, therefore it is a more relaxed atmosphere with skills and drills to develop diamond skills each session and typically divide the kids that are there into a scrimmage game every session. 

Player Pitch for the 8-12 year olds will play on Paramount South, same days and times, so if you have a player on the bubble between the two groups by age and/or skillset (the 8-9 year olds) we can shift them over to the older group if that is preferred without needing to do anything formally, just let one of the coaches there know and if they agree they will send them over to the other field. 

Let's have some fun in the summer SUN!
