Hi Coaches/Volunteers,

While we didn’t kick off the Summer season exactly when we planned, we’re very close to launching a great season!

A few important updates for you:

  • Schedules: Your game schedules should be live in Sports Connect in the next 24 hours. Jason DeMotts will be notifying coaches of teams playing tomorrow/Wednesday of their schedules. Be ready for a quick turnaround - you will need to rally your teams to play with minimal time. Get game communication out to parents ASAP! If you do not get the participation you need from players, please notify us or the opposing coach so we can get additional players or cancel.

  • Uniform Pickup: Uniforms are available on my front porch from 7am to 10pm at 6824 Linden Ave N. Please designate a volunteer to pick them up for your team. Your players will supply their own hats...game shirts are provided for Summer.

  • Game Changer Setup: Be sure to link your Summer Sports Connect team to Game Changer (https://help.gc.com/hc/en-us/articles/360006893592-Importing-Your-Team-From-Sports-Connect). Important: do not create a new team in the app, or you won’t get interleague game updates. Game Changer is a great tool for managing your team on game days.

  • Equipment Needs: If you need gear, baseballs, or other equipment, reach out to Ernesto at equipment@ncllball.com as soon as possible. Some of you may still have gear from the Spring season. If you need additional equipment or replacements, Ernesto is your guy!

Summer Ball is meant to be relaxed and somewhat player-led, but if you know the game, help the kids grow. The game becomes safer and more fun when they learn to play it the right way. Let’s strike a balance: let the kids lead and explore, but don’t hesitate to offer guidance that sets them up for success when Spring baseball rolls back around.

Please let the parents know what they need to bring. Wear baseball clothing + hat, bring water, you will need your own fielder's glove, bats and helmets are provided for those that do not have one, WEAR A CUP!!!

Thanks again for stepping up to volunteer. Your efforts make a huge difference to your players and to the whole NCLL community.
