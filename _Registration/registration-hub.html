Eligibility / League Boundaries

Players qualify for NCLL through one of two paths — only one is required.

  • Residency — live inside the NCLL boundary
  • School enrollment — attend a school inside the boundary

NCLL serves Wallingford, Green Lake, Fremont, Phinney Ridge, Greenwood, and Ballard.

Residency

Players who live inside the official NCLL boundary are eligible. Use the Little League International League Finder Tool to confirm your home league.

School Enrollment

Players enrolled at schools inside the NCLL boundary qualify even if they live outside it. Preschools do not affect eligibility.

Where Fees Go

Your registration fee covers fields, uniforms, equipment, umpires, player development, All-Stars, summerball, and league operations.

  • Fee distribution chart
  • Core operating cost breakdown

Fee Distribution

  • Fields — $46,884 (24.92%)
  • Unis — $53,818 (28.59%)
  • Equipment — $16,907 (8.98%)
  • Umpire — $4,939 (2.62%)
  • Fall/Winter Dev — $4,384 (2.33%)
  • Coach/Player Dev — $8,055 (4.28%)
  • All‑Stars Unis — $21,866 (11.62%)
  • Summerball — $3,449 (1.83%)
  • Overhead — $27,925 (14.84%)

Based on NCLL 2025 budget summary.

Core Operating Costs

  • Fields: permits, rentals, and on‑field materials/maintenance (chalk, bases, mound clay).
  • Uniforms: jerseys, hats/caps, socks (AAA+ also receive hoodies).
  • Equipment: shared gear — catcher’s equipment, helmets, bats, tees, practice balls, first‑aid kits.
  • Umpires: training and per‑game fees where applicable.
  • Fall/Winter development: off-season clinics and skills sessions.
  • Coach/Player development: clinics, assessments, scheduling tools, and in-season development.
  • All‑Stars uniforms: tournament uniform costs.
  • Summerball: support for off‑season programming.
  • Operations & overhead: registration systems, communications, and administration.

Financial Aid

NCLL ensures every child can play baseball or softball regardless of financial situation. No player is turned away for inability to pay.

We offer full and partial scholarships through our confidential need-based assistance program.

Apply for Financial Aid

How to Apply

  • Complete the Financial Aid form before registering.
  • Apply early to ensure processing before deadlines.
  • Approved families receive a promotional code for registration to enter in their cart — sent via email from our Registrar.

What Assistance Covers

  • Registration fees
  • Equipment (when available)
  • Special programs
