Siblings receive a $20 discount per registration after the first child when everyone checks out in the same cart.

Starting in 2024, players moving from A/AA to AAA/Majors receive a custom team hoodie (with jersey number) as part of their uniform kit.

NCLL believes that no one should be denied the ability to play ball due to economic circumstances. Each year, we grant scholarships to families who may need assistance. If you are interested, please fill out the Scholarship Request form or contact our registrar.

Core Operating Costs: Overview

  • Fields: permits, rentals, and on‑field materials/maintenance (chalk, bases, mound clay) to keep diamonds playable.
  • Uniforms: league‑supplied jerseys, hats/caps, socks (AAA+ also receive hoodies).
  • Equipment: shared gear (catcher’s equipment, helmets, bats, tees, practice balls, first‑aid kits).
  • Umpires: training and per‑game fees where applicable.
  • Fall/Winter development: off-season clinics and skills sessions.
  • Coach/Player development: clinics, assessments, scheduling tools, and in-season development.
  • All‑Stars uniforms: tournament uniform costs.
  • Summerball: support for off‑season programming.
  • Operations & overhead: registration systems, communications, administration, and general expenses.
