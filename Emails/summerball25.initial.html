SUMMERBALL25 IS HERE!

Don't let your player's swing get rusty this summer! NCLL's Summerball25 program offers five weeks of laid-back baseball and softball fun from July 7th through August 14th.

The Perfect Summer Program:

  • Affordable - just $75 for the entire season
  • Convenient - TWO games per week (10-12 games total)
  • Relaxed - NO practices, just show up and play!
  • Flexible - ideal for families with summer travel plans
  • Fun-focused - low-pressure environment for all skill levels
  • All-inclusive - program jersey provided with registration

We Need Your Help!

Parent volunteers make Summerball25 possible! Have summer vacation plans? No problem! Even helping with a few games makes a huge difference, and all experience levels are welcome. Simply sign up to volunteer during the same registration process as your player — we've got a role for everyone!
