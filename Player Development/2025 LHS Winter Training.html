Program Overview

One-day, station-based skills camp led by Lincoln High School coaches at Lower Woodland for ages 8–14.

Skills & Focus

An afternoon skills camp led by Lincoln High School coaches (with player mentors) focused on core development. Campers rotate through stations for:

  • Infield fielding.
  • Outfield fundamentals.
  • Hitting mechanics.
  • Pitching & throwing.
  • Baserunning.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • League Ages 8–14 (AA through Seniors).
  • Players from NCLL, Queen Anne LL (QALL), and RUG welcome.
