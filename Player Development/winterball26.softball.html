Program Overview

Winterball26 Softball Training delivers free, high-rep Saturday sessions to get AAA, Majors, Juniors, and AA players (and AA coaches) ready for spring. Join as many dates as you are free; each session is capped at 24 to keep reps high.

Location: Wallingford Boys and Girls Club, 1310 N 45th St, Seattle, WA 98103.

Coaching: Nicola Simpson plus NCLL volunteer staff.

Contacts: Chris Cunningham and Gayle Perez at softball@ncllball.com.

Why This Matters

  • Sharpen skills before Assessments (Feb 7 for AAA and above) with extra reps in January.
  • Free access removes barriers; scholarships are not needed for this program.
  • Small caps keep instruction tight and feedback frequent.

Coach: Nicola Simpson

Nicola is a pitcher and hitter with NCAA Division I and international experience, including the Women's College World Series and SEC titles with Tennessee, and a Pitcher/Utility role at Seattle University. She also works with Seattle Fastpitch (a select/club program - see NCLL's select/club explainer for how these programs work). She has competed for the Canadian Women's Senior National Team with a 1.31 ERA at the World Games.

Highlights: WA State Gatorade Player of the Year (2021), PGF All-American, 498 career strikeouts and 14 no-hitters in high school, NFCA All-Region at Seattle U (2025). Academic honors include CSC Academic All-District and Easton/NFCA Scholar Athlete.

Learn more about Nicola at her Seattle University roster profile.
