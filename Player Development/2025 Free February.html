Program Overview

Get ready for the 2025 season with NCLL's FREE February training program! These outdoor ramp-up sessions are designed specifically for League age 10–12 players to sharpen their skills, build arm strength, and prepare for the upcoming baseball season. Led by experienced NCLL coaches, these clinic-style sessions provide valuable practice time before regular team activities begin.

Coaches & Staff

Our Free February sessions are led by NCLL's dedicated volunteer coaches from the Majors and AAA divisions. These experienced coaches bring years of youth baseball knowledge and are committed to player development at all levels. You'll recognize many of these familiar faces from assessments, regular season games, and practices throughout the year. Their expertise in fundamental skills training and age-appropriate instruction ensures each player receives quality coaching in a supportive environment.

Skills & Focus

  • Build arm strength and reinforce fundamental skills as players prepare for team practices and the upcoming season.
  • Station-based format provides focused instruction similar to our assessment process, ensuring every player gets quality repetitions and coaching feedback.
  • Emphasis on proper mechanics, conditioning, and game-ready skills for AAA and Majors level play.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • These sessions are reserved for players entering or returning to AAA and Majors Baseball Divisions.
  • Players must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season.
