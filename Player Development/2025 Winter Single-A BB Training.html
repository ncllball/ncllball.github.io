Program Overview

This is a fundamentals clinic for Single-A players to prepare for the 2025 season. Coach Mark Linden will take the players and coaches through drills and activities that train the fundamentals of throwing, catching, and fielding.

Coaches & Staff

Experience the difference with Mark Linden of Baseball Positive. Combining 15 years of Little League coaching with his professional playing career, Mark delivers a clinic filled with infectious energy and positive instruction. We strongly encourage you to attend.

Skills & Focus

  • The clinic will focus on throwing, catching, and fielding skills.
  • Coach Linden will take the players through drills and activities that train the fundamentals of throwing, catching, and fielding.
  • The activities are simple and elementary.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season as the medical, code of conduct, and concussion agreements from that registration will apply.
  • Open to all existing and incoming Single-A baseball players.

Dates & Times

The clinic will be held on two Saturdays with each having two one-hour time slots from which to choose:

  • Saturday, March 1st @ 2:00–3:00 PM
  • Saturday, March 1st @ 3:00–4:00 PM
  • Saturday, March 8th @ 2:00–3:00 PM
  • Saturday, March 8th @ 3:00–4:00 PM

Location

This clinic will be held at the Wallingford Boys and Girls Club and welcomes all incoming and returning Single-A players and families.

Cost & Scholarships

  • The cost of the clinic is $20 per session.
  • Scholarships are available upon request.

Registration Information

  • Space is limited to 15 kids per session, so parents are encouraged to sign up as soon as possible.
  • Kids can sign up for more than one session, HOWEVER, please allow others a chance to participate.
  • Please register using your Sports Connect account.
  • A Visa, MasterCard, or Discover card is needed to submit payment.

Important Notes

  • Parents, assistant coaches, and coaches are urged to stay to watch and/or participate in the clinic.
  • All participants will receive an Instruction Guide with the content covered in the program.
  • It is encouraged that parents allow all kids who want to attend to have a chance to participate in at least one of the four sessions.
