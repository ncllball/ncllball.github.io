Program Overview

This is a fundamentals clinic for Single-A players to prepare for the 2025 season. Coach Mark Linden will take the players and coaches through drills and activities that train the fundamentals of throwing, catching, and fielding.

Experience the difference with Mark Linden of Baseball Positive. Drawing on 15 years of Little League coaching plus a professional playing career, he brings infectious energy, clear progressions, and relentlessly positive instruction. We strongly encourage you to attend.

Skills & Focus

  • Foundational throwing, catching, and fielding mechanics.
  • Progressive, high-repetition small-area drills that reinforce proper footwork and glove work.
  • Simple, scalable activities coaches can re-use during the season.

Eligibility

  • Open to all existing and incoming Single-A baseball players.
  • Must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season as the medical, code of conduct, and concussion agreements from that registration will apply.
