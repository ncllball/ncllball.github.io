Program Overview

This program delivers fun, age-appropriate practices advancing core skills: pitching, hitting, and athletic movement. Designed to:

  • Group players by age/ability/availability
  • Run weekly from the week of Nov 3 through late January (before assessments)
  • Provide consistent indoor conditioning and batting reps

Coaches & Staff

  • Upcoming AAA and Majors coaches support winter sessions
  • Each group has a designated lead coach
  • Parent Assistants needed to help keep stations moving—indicate interest at registration

Skills & Focus

  • Extend development window before spring
  • Prepare players for a successful Little League season
  • Extra pitching & hitting reps that are hard to fit in-season
  • Offer growth without select-level commitments

Dates & Times

  • Indoor training (see Location)
  • Session day options: Monday evening, Thursday evening, or a Weekend session (time TBD) — each is a separate offering you can choose from during the registration process
  • First practice — Week of Nov 3rd
