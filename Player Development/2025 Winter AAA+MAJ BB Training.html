What is it

Provide fun, age-appropriate practices designed to advance players' skills in three areas crucial to baseball success -- pitching, hitting, and strong athletic movement. This program is designed to:

  • Kids will be split into groups based on age/ability/times available.
  • The training will begin the week of November 4th and run through January.
  • We will meet weekly indoors and focus on conditioning and batting.

Coaches & Volunteers

  • Coaches for our upcoming AAA and Majors seasons have agreed to support our winter training.
  • Each session will have a coach to lead and organize the group.
  • Parent Assistants - We will need to have a parent assistant for each practice group to assist with keeping the kids moving through drills. When you enroll, please mark your interest in helping out.

Clinic Focus

  • Extend the baseball training season for motivated players.
  • Ready AAA or Majors players for successful Little League seasons.
  • Ensure that young players have a chance to learn advanced techniques and get many reps hitting and pitching, which are challenging to do in-season.
  • Allow athletes room to grow without requiring extensive commitments like select baseball.

Locations and Times

  • Indoor training will be at The Hive on Elliot Ave near the Expedia Hub on Monday, Friday, or Sunday evenings or Focus Cages on Aurora Ave on Thursday evenings.
  • First practice - Week of November 4th.
  • Last practice - Week of 1/27, wrapping up before NCLL assessments.
  • No practices on the following weeks: 11/25 (Thanksgiving), 12/23, and 12/30 (Winter Break).

FAQ

  • What should my child bring to practice? Your child should bring a glove, bat, helmet, and water bottle.
  • What is the refund policy? Refunds will be provided up to two weeks before the start of the program.
  • Are there any prerequisites? No, this program is open to all players in the AAA and Majors divisions.
