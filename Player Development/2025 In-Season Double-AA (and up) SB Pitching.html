What is it?

North Central Little League (NCLL) is proud to offer free in-season pitching training for NCLL softball players at the AA level and above.

About Coaches

We are excited to have Hunter Mullally, local coach at Bishop Blanchet High School and Seattle Fastpitch Club team joining NCLL to lead these sessions.

NOTE: Please see our guide for more information on Club/Select/Travel teams.

Clinic Focus

These clinics are designed to help players develop and refine their pitching skills under the guidance of Hunter Mullally. Whether your child is looking to improve their technique or boost their confidence on the mound — this is a great opportunity to receive expert coaching in a fun and supportive environment.

Eligibility Information

  • Must be an NCLL softball player at the AA level and above.
  • Must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season Softball Registration
