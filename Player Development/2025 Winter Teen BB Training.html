Program Overview

We are excited to offer a specialized off-season training program for teenage baseball players (league age 13-16) looking to enhance their skills and prepare for the upcoming Little League and High School seasons.

This is especially recommended for teen players entering or preparing for high school—come get those 'reps' in!

This registration session will allow you to complete contact information, sign up for participation, and submit payment for the 2025 NCLL Winter Training for Teen Baseball.

Skills & Focus

The TEEN off-season development program is an opportunity for teenage players (league age 13-16) to receive additional training and reps during the off-season. This hitting training will be led by legendary coach Ray Atkinson at Focus Baseball Facility (7806 Aurora Ave N, Seattle, Washington 98103).

NOTE: There will be two Winter Training for Teen Baseball programs in 2025:

  • Winter Training I will run from November 9th to December 17th.
  • Winter Training II will run from January 4th to February 22nd.
