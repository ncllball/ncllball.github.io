Program Overview

We’re excited to offer a specialized off-season training program for teenage baseball players (league age 13–16) looking to enhance their skills and prepare for the upcoming Little League and high school seasons.

This is especially recommended for players entering or preparing for high school—come get those reps in!

This registration will collect contact information, your participation choice, and payment for the 2025 NCLL Winter Teen Baseball Training.

Skills & Focus

The teen off-season development program is an opportunity for players (league age 13–16) to receive additional training and reps during the winter. This hitting training will be led by legendary coach Ray Atkinson at Focus Baseball Indoor Facility (7806 Aurora Ave N, Seattle, WA 98103).

Note: There will be two Winter Training for Teen Baseball programs in 2025:

  • Winter Training I will run from November 9th to December 17th.
  • Winter Training II will run from January 4th to February 22nd. (Dates listed below; year transition assumed.)
