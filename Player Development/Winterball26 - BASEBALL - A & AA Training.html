Program Overview

Let’s get ready for baseball season! Join Mark Linden of Baseball Positive for a fundamentals clinic for A Baseball and AA Baseball. Mark focuses on throwing, catching, and fielding through fun drills and activities.

  • Great warm-up before the season begins
  • Instruction guide provided to all participants
  • Coaches, assistant coaches, and parents are encouraged to stay and participate

Why This Matters

  • Sharpen skills and learn fundamentals before the spring season begins
  • Small-group training with focused instruction and extra reps
  • Coach Mark runs simple, positive drills that build confidence for players and parents
  • Our goal is to get kids excited and ready for the baseball season

Skills & Focus

  • Foundational throwing, catching, and fielding mechanics
  • Progressive, high-repetition drills that reinforce proper footwork and glove work
  • Simple, scalable activities coaches can re-use during the season

Coach: Mark Linden & Baseball Positive

Former NCAA D-I head coach and professional player Mark Linden leads these clinics. He has spent the past 17 years focused on youth player development. Baseball Positive provides a coaching system focused on structure, efficiency, and simple fundamentals.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Open to all existing and incoming A and AA baseball players
  • Must be registered for the 2026 Regular Season as the medical, code of conduct, and concussion agreements carry over from that registration

Registration

  • $20 per session (scholarships available upon request).
  • Complete registration for A Baseball or AA Baseball session blocks
  • If a session is full, complete registration to join the waitlist
  • Space is limited to 15 kids per session, so parents are encouraged to sign up early
  • Players may register for more than one session, but please allow others a chance to participate
  • Requires Sports Connect account (create if needed)
  • Have Visa / MasterCard / American Express ready
