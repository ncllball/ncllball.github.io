Program Overview

Calling all softball players from 3rd to 12th grade! Whether you're tuning up for Little League, preparing for the spring select season, or gearing up for high school tryouts, the Roosevelt Winter Batting Clinic is here to help you sharpen your skills and step up your game.

Led by Head Coach HT Lee, a staff of experienced assistant coaches, and veteran Roosevelt High School players, this clinic offers a fun, safe, and supportive environment for players at every skill level. Whether you're just learning how to swing a bat or looking for a quick tune-up before tryouts, this clinic has something for everyone!

What to Expect

  • Warmup routines
  • Hitting off the tee
  • Soft toss
  • Coach-pitch drills
  • Pitching machine
