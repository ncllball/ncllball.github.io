NCLL coaches,

As we approach winter, we’re excited to bring back our Winterball program. Over the past few seasons, this training has made a notable difference in the readiness of our AAA and Majors players.

Quick note: We still need Winter Training Parent Helpers/Assistants and Winter Training Head Coach. More information can be found here.

Program Details

  • Goals: hitting, fielding, and conditioning
  • Dates: November–January (no sessions during holiday breaks)
  • Session Duration: 60 or 90 minutes
  • Locations: The Hive (921 Elliott Ave W) and Focus Cages (7806 Aurora Ave N)
  • Age Group: players slated for AAA & Majors in Spring 2026
  • Group Size: 8–10 players per session (max 12)

We plan to offer sessions on Monday evening (The Hive), Thursday evening (Focus), Friday evening (The Hive), and Saturday morning (The Hive), subject to coach availability.

Coaching Duties

  • Training & Skill Development: lead drills focusing on hitting, fielding, and core techniques; provide feedback and encouragement
  • Planning: prepare each training session to maximize engagement and development; resources available upon request
  • Safety & Supervision: ensure safe activities and appropriate supervision at all times
  • Team Management & Collaboration: communicate schedule updates to families; open/close facilities as needed; coordinate with other coaches
