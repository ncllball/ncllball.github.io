Program Overview

Specialized pitching and catching training led by Blanchet High School coach, Hunter Mullally. These sessions are designed to sharpen your skills and prepare for AAA & Majors Assessments, scheduled for February 8.

Assessments are Mandatory for all AAA & Majors players to form teams for the 2025 season. A separate sign-up link will be provided for assessments.

What to Expect

  • Arrive early: Please arrive 10 minutes before start time for check-in and setup.
  • Warm-up: Dynamic stretching and throwing progression led by coaches (10-15 minutes).
  • Stations: Rotations covering hitting, fielding/footwork, and throwing mechanics.
  • Pitching/Catching tracks: Small-group work with focused instruction (including sessions led by Coach Hunter Mullaly).
  • Game-speed drills: Situation reps and competitions to build confidence and decision-making.
  • Cool-down & wrap-up: Light recovery, quick Q&A, and cleanup of the space.
  • Facility reminders: Indoor gym - no cleats. Water only on the court. Respect posted rules.
  • Equipment: Glove and water required. Catchers bring gear if available; limited loaners may be on hand.
