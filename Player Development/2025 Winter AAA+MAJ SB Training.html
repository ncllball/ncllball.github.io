'25 Winter Sofbtall AAA/MAJORS

Join NCLL coaches for weekly training to gear up for the 2025 Softball season! NCLL is proud to sponsor specialized pitching and catching training led by Blanchett High School coach, Hunter Mullaly. These sessions are designed to sharpen your skills and prepare for AAA & Majors Assessments, scheduled for February 8.

Training Details

  • Location: Wallingford Boys & Girls Club, 1310 N 45th St, Seattle, WA 98103
  • What to Bring: Wear tennis shoes, and bring your glove and water. Enter through the gym door on 46th Street.

  • Saturday, January 18, 2025: 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday, January 25, 2025: 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday, February 1, 2025: 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM

Important Notes
