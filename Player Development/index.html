Winter Baseball Programs

Winterball25 AA Baseball Training

Foundational throwing, catching, and fielding; high-engagement fundamental reps.

  • Dates: Mar 1 & 8, 2025
  • Division: Baseball (Single-A)
  • Cost: $20 / session
  • Status: Closed

What Players Build

  • Throwing progression that locks in grip, footwork, and follow-through for AA players.
  • Catching and fielding fundamentals with high rep counts in small groups.
  • Confidence in game-speed routines before teams head outdoors.

Coaching Approach

  • Led by NCLL AA division coaches with station rotations to keep athletes moving.
  • Emphasis on positive reinforcement and teaching players how to practice on their own.

Schedule & Logistics

  • Saturday sessions on Mar 1 & Mar 8, 2025.
  • Slots offered at 2:00–3:00 PM and 3:00–4:00 PM to accommodate team schedules.
  • $20 per session covers facility rental and instructor staffing.

Winterball26 AAA/Majors Baseball Training

Weekly small-group indoor work preparing players for upper-division competition.

  • Dates: Nov 4 - Jan 27
  • Division: Baseball (AAA & Majors)
  • Cost: $250 / player
  • Status: Open

Training Focus

  • Movement prep and sequencing to support stronger throws, swings, and defensive transitions.
  • Upper-division hitting work that layers tee reps, front toss, and machine velocity progression.
  • Conditioning circuits that keep winter reps high without overloading arms.

Format

  • Small-group stations so players rotate through cages, defense, and movement blocks.
  • Led by NCLL player development instructors focused on upper-division readiness.

Schedule & Enrollment

  • Weekly indoor sessions running Nov 4 – Jan 27.
  • Designed for returning AAA and Majors players targeting 2026 competitive play.
  • Program fee: $250 per player covers all sessions, facility time, and instruction.

Due to limited small-group slots, we recommend registering early through the NCLL site.
