Youtaah Umpire Program

NCLL's Youth Umpire Program aims to inspire young individuals, aged 13 to 19, to become umpires. Those who are 14 or older can earn $30 per game ($40 per game for plate umpires, with approval from the UiC or umpire coordinators), or they can opt for community service hours instead.

If you have any older children, nieces/nephews, neighbors, etc. who might be interested, please encourage them to come to the trainings and join Team Blue.
