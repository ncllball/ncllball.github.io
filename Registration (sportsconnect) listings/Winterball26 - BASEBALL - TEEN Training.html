About the Program

Our Teen Off-Season Development Program is tailored for players aged 13-16 (league age) to receive additional training and practice during the off-season. This program is ideally scheduled to prepare players for the upcoming Little League and High School seasons.

The hitting sessions will be led by legendary coach Ray Atkinson at Focus Baseball Facility. These are the only remaining scheduled TEEN training sessions before the 2025 Season!
