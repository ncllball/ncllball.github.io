About the Program

This is our 5th year offering fun, age‑appropriate, station-based practices focused on three core pillars: pitching foundations, quality hitting reps, and athletic movement. Designed to:

  • Extend the training window into winter
  • Prepare AAA & Majors players for a successful Little League season
  • Provide extra pitching and hitting reps that are hard to get in-season
  • Support growth without the time/travel demands of select baseball

Schedule & Format

Program runs weekly from the week of Nov 4 through the week of Jan 27 (wraps before assessments).

  • No sessions: Week of Nov 25 (Thanksgiving), weeks of Dec 23 & Dec 30 (Winter Break)
  • Players grouped by age / ability / availability
  • Indoor conditioning + hitting focus each week

Session Day Options (Choose One)

  • Monday Evening – The Hive (Elliott Ave)
  • Thursday Evening – Focus Cages (Aurora Ave)
  • Friday Evening – The Hive (Elliott Ave)
  • Saturday Morning – The Hive (Elliott Ave)
  • Cost: $260 / player (single weekly session)

Select ONE preferred day during registration. We will contact you if that option is full to discuss alternatives.
