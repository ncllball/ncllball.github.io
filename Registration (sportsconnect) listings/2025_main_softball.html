Welcome to the 2025 Softball Regular Season Registration!!!

This registration process will guide you through:

  • Setting up an account with NCLL and Sports Connect (Be sure to use the same email you use with Gamechanger!)1
  • Signing your children up to participate in the 2025 NCLL Softball Season
  • Signing yourself up as a volunteer for the 2025 NCLL Softball Season2
  • Submitting a Medical Release for your child for the 2025 NCLL Softball Season3
  • Submitting payment

1Gamechanger/Sports Connect integration requires the same emails be used to manage rosters.

2Remember this is a yearly process and please be ready to upload a form of identification!

3For 2025, Medical Releases are built into the registration, unlike previous years. You will digitally sign for it. The coaches will have the same information as always.

Need more instructions? Want a refresher on our divisions? If you're unsure how league age is determined or where your child falls within NCLL, check our league age chart. For details about registration fees, visit our fee information page. For any registration-related inquiries, feel free to contact NCLL's registrar.
