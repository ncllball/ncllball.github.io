Navigating the world of youth sports can be challenging for parents. As a double-goal league, NCLL aims to support you in supporting your child.

## [Positive Coaching Alliance](http://www.positivecoach.org/)

Our partner offers resources to help parents understand how to work with coaches, support their children after tough games, and practice positive sports parenting.

## [I Love to Watch You Play](http://ilovetowatchyouplay.com/)

Articles specifically for sports parents. Based on the phrase children find most supportive from their parents after a game: *"I love to watch you play."*

## [Changing the Game Project](http://www.changingthegameproject.com/)

Highlights the potential negative effects of the overly competitive nature of youth sports. Advocates for developmentally appropriate youth sports for leagues, programs, coaches, and parents.
