## Positive Coaching

NCLL is a double-goal league, meaning managers and coaches are trained by the Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA). PCA focuses on developing the whole athlete, using sports to teach life lessons, and supporting coaches and parents to think beyond wins and losses — to think about the kind of person you want your child to become.

- [Positive Coaching Alliance](http://www.positivecoach.org/) — resources for coaches, parents, and athletes. Coaches can sign up for weekly email reminders during the season.

## Sample Parent/Guardian Meeting Agenda

A pre-season parent meeting is a wise investment. People tend to live up to expectations when they know them. Most effective in a private setting (a coach's or parent's home) where you have full attention. Can also be scheduled before one of the first practices.

## Safety

NCLL managers and coaches always err on the side of caution. All managers are trained on the NCLL Safety Plan and concussion protocol.
