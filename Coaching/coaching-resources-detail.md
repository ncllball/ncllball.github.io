Managers and coaches are asked to play many roles throughout the season. Here are some resources to help.

## Positive Coaching

NCLL is a double-goal league — managers and coaches are trained yearly by the [Positive Coaching Alliance (PCA)](http://www.positivecoach.org/). PCA promotes an approach to youth sports that focuses on:

- Developing the whole athlete
- Using sports to teach life lessons
- Supporting coaches and parents to think beyond wins and losses

PCA provides resources for coaches, parents, and athletes — including videos, research, and weekly email reminders during the season.

## Pre-Season Parent/Guardian Meeting

People tend to live up to expectations when they know them. A pre-season parent meeting helps set the tone and communicate important information.

**When/where:** Most effective in a private setting with everyone's full attention. Can also be scheduled before one of the first practices.

**What to cover:**
- Introductions — yourself and any other coaches/team officials
- Team goals for the season, on and off the field
- Expectations for players and parents (behavior, attendance, communication)
- Practice and game schedule
- How you'll communicate throughout the season
- Q&A
