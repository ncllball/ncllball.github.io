Summerball26 is NCLL’s summer baseball and softball program — open to any player, no geographic or school boundaries. No practices, just games with friends over six weeks in July and August.

Registration is open June 6–30. Interleague teams are built with friend requests as a top priority (not guaranteed) and announced around July 4. Sandlot divisions have no fixed rosters — game-day instructions are sent instead. Have questions? The Summerball FAQ has answers.

General Information

  • Game Schedule: 2 games per week, 10–12 total
  • Registration Fee: $75 per player (scholarships available by request before registering)
  • Uniform: Game jersey provided; wear spring season cap or any cap
  • Required Equipment: Glove, cleats or sneakers; protective cup required for baseball
  • Optional Equipment: Personal bat; shared helmets available

Baseball

Baseball Division Guide

Register for the division your player expects to play in Spring ’27 — ask yourself: Where will my player play in Spring ’27? If there is even a chance, then give it a go! If unsure between two levels, register for the higher one and email summerball@ncllball.com.

Your options this summer: Baseball A (Sandlot) — flexible, parent-run format for players out of Kindy Coach Pitch or Single-A. AA through Juniors — set teams, regular schedules, competitive interleague play.

* Summerball division choice does not commit or entitle you to a Spring ’27 placement — to better understand where your player lands next spring, see Step 2(a): Division Information.

Why Play Up in Summerball26 ?

Here’s why it makes for better Summerball — and a stronger spring season:

  1. Better Competition — Local leagues have agreed to align divisions based on where players will be in Spring of ’27, creating balanced teams
  2. Low-Stakes, High-Growth — All-Stars are away at tournaments — perfect time to safely build confidence at the new level
  3. Head Start for Spring ’27 — Early reps now means hitting the ground running in Spring ’27

Baseball A (Sandlot)

For players who played Kindy Coach Pitch or Single-A in Spring 2026 and will be in Baseball A in Spring 2027. Summerball doesn’t offer a T-ball division — Baseball A is the youngest available. Moving up to AA? Register for that Summerball division instead.

Having many parent volunteers is the only way we can continue to offer Baseball A during Summerball.

Summerball26 VOLUNTEERS

Summerball runs on parent volunteers — we need coaches and team managers for baseball divisions. The bigger our crew, the easier it is to cover for each other. It’s summer — take your vacation, someone’s got it.

Sign up via the Volunteer Hub — tap through for your options:

  • Lead Game Volunteer
  • Game Day Helper
  • Team Manager
  • Sandlot Team Organizer (Baseball A)

Lead Game Volunteer

  • Bring team gear, help with setup, ensure safety, and handle unusual situations on game day
  • Majors and older run themselves — they just need you there

Game Day Helper

  • Dugout manager, lineup help, base coach, umpire, or parent pitcher for AA Baseball
  • Baseball A and Baseball AA are where you’ll shine
  • AAA & up players may coach bases (helmet required)

Team Manager

  • Organize RSVPs, send game reminders, keep families in the loop
  • Perfect if you love organizing — no baseball experience needed

Sandlot Team Organizer (Baseball A)

  • Organize players, balance teams, keep it fun, oversee safety
  • Perfect for a low-key, go-with-the-flow game day

How to Sign Up

Sign up at the Volunteer Hub — your coordinator will follow up before the season starts.
