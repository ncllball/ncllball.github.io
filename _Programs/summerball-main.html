Summerball26 is NCLL’s summer baseball and softball program — open to any player, no geographic or school boundaries. No practices, just games with friends over six weeks in July and August.

Registration is open June 6–30. Interleague teams are sorted by friend requests and announced around July 4; sandlot divisions are looser, drop-in formats. Have questions? The Summerball FAQ has answers.

Summerball26 Program

Why Summerball26 ?

  • Flexible schedule — no guilt, players and coaches alike!
  • No practices, just games!
  • Interleague teams are sorted by friends — list yours at registration! Sandlot stays flexible and drop-in.
  • No zip codes, no school zones — anyone can play!
  • Low-pressure, fun baseball & softball!
  • Just $75 for a full summer of games — great value, great baseball!

  • Registration Fee: $75 per player (scholarships available)
  • Uniform: Game shirt provided; wear spring season cap or any cap
  • Required Equipment: Glove, cleats or sneakers; protective cup required for baseball
  • Optional Equipment: Personal bat; shared helmets available

Summerball26 VOLUNTEERS

Summerball runs on parent volunteers — we need coaches and team managers for baseball divisions. The bigger our crew, the easier it is to cover for each other. It’s summer — take your vacation, someone’s got it.

During registration you’ll be asked if you’d like to volunteer — tap through for your options:

  • Lead Game Volunteer
  • Game Day Helper
  • Team Manager
  • Sandlot Team Organizer (A Baseball)

Lead Game Volunteer

  • Bring team gear, help with setup, ensure safety
  • Handle unusual situations on game day
  • Majors and older run themselves — they just need you there
  • Advanced players may coach bases (helmet required)

Game Day Helper

  • Dugout manager, lineup help, base coach, umpire, or parent pitcher for AA Baseball
  • AA/AAA Baseball and A Baseball Sandlot are where you’ll shine
  • AAA and up players may coach bases (with supervision)

Team Manager

  • Organize RSVPs, send game reminders, keep families in the loop
  • Perfect if you love organizing — no baseball experience needed

Sandlot Team Organizer (A Baseball)

  • Organize players, balance teams, keep it fun, oversee safety
  • Perfect for a low-key, go-with-the-flow game day

Scroll down to the registration form to sign up — you’ll be prompted to select your preferred volunteer role, and your coordinator will follow up before the season starts.

Baseball (Interleague)

AA, AAA, Majors, and Juniors/TEEN are separate divisions — no mixing across age groups. Set teams, regular schedules, competitive play against neighboring leagues.

  • Play at the level you’ll be in Spring ’27
  • AA Baseball may include a sandlot/interleague hybrid
  • Volunteer coaches needed — all are welcome!

Participation in Summerball does not commit or entitle you to a Spring ’27 division placement — divisions will be assigned based on league age and skill assessed at that time.

Why Play Up in Summerball26 ?

  1. Better Competition — Local leagues have agreed to align divisions based on where players will be in Spring of ’27, creating balanced teams
  2. Low-Stakes, High-Growth — All-Stars are away at tournaments — perfect time to safely build confidence at the new level
  3. Head Start for Spring ’27 — Early reps now means hitting the ground running in Spring ’27

Softball Division Guidance

  • AA/AAA → Softball AA/AAA (Sandlot) — League Age 6–8
  • AAA/MAJ → Softball AAA/MAJ (Sandlot) — League Age 8–11; experienced AAA or returning Majors

Participation in Summerball does not commit or entitle you to a Spring ’27 division placement — divisions will be assigned based on league age and skill assessed at that time.

Baseball Division Guidance

  • Kindy Coach Pitch / A → A (Sandlot) — League Age 7–8; must have completed KCP or Single-A
  • AA → AA (Interleague/Sandlot) — League Age 8–9; strong 9s may try AAA
  • AAA → AAA (Interleague) — League Age 9–11; strong 10s may play Majors
  • Majors → Majors (Interleague) — League Age 11–12; 11s may stay in AAA
  • Juniors/TEEN → Juniors/TEEN (Interleague) — League Age 13–15
