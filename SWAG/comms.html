Hello North Central Little League families and friends!

Over the last couple of years, some of NCLL's long time community members and volunteers had advocated for designing and adopting a new logo with an identity unique to NCLL. We needed something inclusive of baseball and softball, and we wanted it to represent a place we all know so well.

The Cloverleaf at Lower Woodland, NCLL's home fields.

As players grow up from Tee Ball through Single-A to Double-AA, the Cloverleaf becomes the "show" they can't wait to play at. We have spent countless evenings and weekends there to support our players in one way or another, from cheering from the bleachers to coaching and umpiring on the field. It is a special place we all love and will all be able to easily identify in our new logo. We can’t wait to start seeing it worn with pride wherever we go!

The new logo is rolling out now starting with gear for coaches, volunteers, and players. We are also excited to announce the launch of our SWAG Store! 10% of all purchases from the store will help fund scholarships, player development, and various initiatives to support our league. You can even find your player's team apparel equipped with their sponsor logo in TEES, HOODIES, and HATS. Order now as you'll need to allow 4 weeks for delivery! Be sure to follow us on social media for chances to win NCLL merch!

Thank you for being a part of the North Central Little League family. Together, let’s make this season one to remember.

See you at the Cloverleaf!

— Board of Directors
