Winterball25 Baseball AA Pitching Training

2025 Winter Double-AA Baseball Pitching NCLL
  • Dates: Feb 22, Mar 1 & 8, 2025
  • Location: The Hive
  • Time: 12:00 PM or 1:15 PM
  • Cost: $60 / player
  • Division(s): Baseball (AA)
  • Led by: Friends of NCLL Northwest Sting Baseball

Program Overview

Is your AA baseball player ready to take the mound this season? Do you like shorter games because the kids are nailing the strike zone? If so, let's do our part and make sure theyre prepared to take the mound this season!

NCLL is excited to offer a specialized pitching clinic tailored specifically for AA baseball players looking to pitch.

Your player will be guided by a member of NORTHWEST STING BASEBALLS coaching staff, all of whom have extensive pitching experience as both instructors and players. Parents, coaches, and assistant coaches are welcome to stay, watch, or participate in these sessions.

Coaches & Staff

Northwest Sting is a local club baseball program that focuses on youth athletic development. Led by Dashawn Patrick, the program aims to provide players with opportunities to improve their baseball skills while also emphasizing academic performance and community involvement. Northwest Sting offers a competitive environment designed to foster a strong work ethic and a focus on achieving goals.

Please see our parent's guide to club/select/travel teams to learn more

Skills & Focus

This clinic aims to provide AA baseball players with a strong foundation in pitching fundamentals, preparing them for success in the 2025 season and beyond.

Help your player build confidence, refine their pitching skills, and set the stage for a successful season with this opportunity!

This three-week clinic includes three one-hour sessions, with each session building upon the last. Players will focus on:

  • Proper pitching form
  • Grip techniques
  • Arm care

Whether theyre just getting started or looking to elevate their game, this clinic will provide the necessary tools and knowledge.

Eligibility & Requirements

All participants must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season BASEBALL at the AA level. If your child isnt yet registered, please complete the regular season registration before enrolling in this clinic.
