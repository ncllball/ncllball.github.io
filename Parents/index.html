Uniform Sizing —Players & Coaches

A & AA players receive jersey model 4003 with the adjustable 4301 cap, plus league-supplied socks and belt (OSFA). Coaches at both levels use the ST350 top with the same hat. Order carefully — sizes are final at delivery and reorders are billed to your account. Use the normalized pit-to-pit charts on the panels to choose the best fit.
