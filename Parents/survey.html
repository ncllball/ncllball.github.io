Baseball League Photo Vendor Satisfaction Survey

A Note from Our Volunteer League:

Please remember that our baseball league is entirely run by volunteers and operates with limited resources. Your honest feedback on this survey is invaluable as we strive to improve our services for our players and families, always keeping in mind what we can reasonably accomplish as a volunteer-led league.

Please rate your satisfaction with the following aspects of our photo vendor's service, using a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is "Very Dissatisfied" and 5 is "Very Satisfied."

1. Overall Quality of Photos:

How satisfied are you with the overall quality of the team and individual photos (e.g., clarity, composition, lighting)?

2. Photographer's Professionalism and Interaction:

How satisfied are you with the photographer's professionalism and how well they interacted with the players during the photo session?

3. Ease of Ordering Process:

How satisfied are you with the ease and clarity of the photo ordering process?

4. Timeliness of Delivery:

How satisfied are you with the time it took to receive the photos after they were ordered?

5. Value for Money:

How satisfied are you with the value you received for the price paid for the photos?

6. Variety of Offerings (Poses & Products):

How satisfied are you with the variety of poses, photo packages, and products offered by the vendor?

7. Likelihood to Recommend:

How likely are you to recommend this photo vendor to another sports league or team? (1 = Very Unlikely, 5 = Very Likely)
