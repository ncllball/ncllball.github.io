NCLL provides nearly all equipment required for the season. However, players may bring their own — if so, please mark everything clearly with the player's name.

## Recommended Clothing

- Baseball pants or sweatpants (to protect legs)
- Cleats or sneakers — molded plastic only, no metal cleats
- Warm clothes for early-season practices
- Clothing that can withstand dirt and wear

## Required Player-Provided Equipment

- **Glove (mitt)** — Players under 10 usually do best with a youth-sized glove. Players 11+ usually do best with an 11.5" glove. The Mizuno Prospect series is great for age 10 and under.
- **Protective cup** (boys)
- **Pants** — Gray baseball pants suggested for baseball; black softball pants for softball. Tee Ball players do not need to purchase baseball pants.

## League-Provided Equipment

Players may bring their own if they prefer:

- Bats — must be [Little League approved](https://www.littleleague.org/playing-rules/bat-rules/) with a USA Baseball insignia on the stem
- Catcher's gear
- Helmets

Need help with equipment costs? Contact the registrar about financial assistance.
