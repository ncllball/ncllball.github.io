NCLL Alumni

Once a Cardinal, always a Cardinal. If you played or volunteered at NCLL, we’d love to stay in touch and share ways to give back — even a small gift makes a difference

Stay connected

  • Join the alumni list: email us
  • Tell us your story: championships, coaches, and favorite moments
  • Volunteer for a day: Opening Day, Photo Day, Home Run Derby

Ways alumni can help

  • Donate via Venmo — funds go to fields, gear, and scholarships. Try “Give $26 for 2026”
  • Company match — check your employer’s matching program
  • Mentor or coach — help today’s players learn the game
  • Sponsor a team — recognition for your business and a big boost for the league
