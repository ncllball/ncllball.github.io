What is it?

Is your AA baseball player ready to take the mound this season? Do you like shorter games because the kids are nailing the strike zone? If so, let's do our part and make sure they’re prepared to take the mound this season!

NCLL is excited to offer a specialized pitching clinic tailored specifically for AA baseball players looking to pitch.

Your player will be guided by a member of NORTHWEST STING BASEBALL’S coaching staff, all of whom have extensive pitching experience as both instructors and players. Parents, coaches, and assistant coaches are welcome to stay, watch, or participate in these sessions.

About Sting Baseball

Northwest Sting is a local club baseball program that focuses on youth athletic development. Led by Dashawn Patrick, the program aims to provide players with opportunities to improve their baseball skills while also emphasizing academic performance and community involvement. Northwest Sting offers a competitive environment designed to foster a strong work ethic and a focus on achieving goals.

Clinic Focus

This clinic aims to provide AA baseball players with a strong foundation in pitching fundamentals, preparing them for success in the 2025 season and beyond.

Help your player build confidence, refine their pitching skills, and set the stage for a successful season with this incredible opportunity!

This three-week clinic includes three one-hour sessions, with each session building upon the last. Players will focus on:

  • Proper pitching form
  • Grip techniques
  • Arm care

Whether they’re just getting started or looking to elevate their game, this clinic will provide the necessary tools and knowledge.
