What is it?

NCLL is offering FREE outdoor ramp-up training sessions for 10, 11 and 12 year old players.

About Coaches

These sessions will be run by our own NCLL volunteer Majors and AAA coaches, many of which you'll see at assessments and in the dugout during the regular season.

Clinic Focus

  • The goal of these sessions is to get reps and build arm strength and fundamentals heading into NCLL team practices.
  • The program will be station-based and run similarly to assessments.

Eligibility Information

  • These sessions are reserved for players entering or returning to AAA and Majors Baseball Divisions.
  • Players must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season.

Dates & Times

We will offer a total of 6 sessions—two 1-hour sessions on each of the three Saturdays listed below:
