What is it?

NCLL is offering FREE outdoor ramp-up training sessions for 10, 11 and 12 year old players.

About Coaches

These sessions will be run by our own NCLL volunteer Majors and AAA coaches, many of which you'll see at assessments and in the dugout during the regular season.

Clinic Focus

  • The goal of these sessions is to get reps and build arm strength and fundamentals heading into NCLL team practices.
  • The program will be station-based and run similarly to assessments.

Eligibility Information

  • These sessions are reserved for players entering or returning to AAA and Majors Baseball Divisions.
  • Must be registered for the 2025 Regular Season Baseball Registration

Dates & Times

We will offer 6 sessions throughout the season, with two 1-hour sessions each Sunday:

  • Saturday, February 1st — 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM, 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM
  • Saturday, February 8th — 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM, 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM
  • Saturday, February 22nd — 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM, 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Location

  • The turf fields at Robert Eagle Staff Middle School

Cost

**FREE**

Registration Information

  • To register, please hit the link below to reserve your spot. We look forward to seeing our players at the clinic!
  • Slots are available on first-come, first-served basis, so be sure to sign up early!
  • Sessions are rain or shine, unless weather becomes a safety concern (e.g. pouring rain, lightning).
