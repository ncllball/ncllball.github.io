North Central Little League 2025 All-Stars Information

Are you ready to represent North Central Little League (NCLL) on the big stage? Each year, NCLL proudly sponsors All-Star teams to compete in the prestigious Little League District 8 Tournament. These teams showcase the best of our league and may even advance to the state tournament!

If your player is interested in being part of this exciting opportunity, don't miss the chance to apply. Communication from coaches should come this week. To be considered, please complete and return the 2025 All-Star Application to your coach by 9:00 p.m. on Thursday, May 15.

Who Can Play?

Baseball

  • 10U: 8/9/10-year-olds
  • 11U: 9/10/11-year-olds
  • 12U: 10/11/12-year-olds
  • Juniors: 13–14-year-olds
  • Seniors: 15–16-year-olds

Softball

  • 10U: 8/9/10-year-olds
  • 11U: 9/10/11-year-olds
  • 12U: 10/11/12-year-olds
  • Juniors: 13–14-year-olds

Key Dates to Remember

  • May 5: League email sent to families with All-Star information and application
  • May 15: Deadline to submit Player Applications (9:00 p.m.)
  • May 16: Baseball All-Star Tryouts (6:00–9:00 p.m. at Lower Woodland Field #3 or #4)
  • May 23: Baseball All-Star Team Formation Completed
  • June 2: Baseball All-Stars Workouts Begin
  • June 18: Softball 12U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins
  • June 19: Softball 11U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins
  • June 26: Baseball 12U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins
  • June 27: Softball 10U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins
  • July 5: Baseball 11U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins
  • July 6: Baseball 10U District 8 All-Star Tournament Begins

How to Apply

To be considered for an NCLL All-Stars team, you must complete the official 2025 All-Star Player Application.

Deadline: Thursday, May 15, by 9:00 p.m.

Download the Application Here

If you cannot return the application to your coach in person, you may scan and email it to:

  • Baseball: baseball@ncllball.com
  • Softball: softball@ncllball.com

Why Join All-Stars?

Being part of an All-Stars team is a unique and rewarding experience. Don't miss this chance to be part of something special. Apply today!
