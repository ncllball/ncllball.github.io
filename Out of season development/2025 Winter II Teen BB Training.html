Welcome to NCLL's Winter Training II for Teen Baseball!

This is a fantastic opportunity to gear up for the upcoming Little League and High School seasons. It's especially recommended for teen players entering or preparing for high school—come get those 'reps' in!

This registration session will guide you through completing contact information, signing up for participation, and submitting payment for the 2025 Winter Training II for Teen Baseball.

About the Program

The TEEN off-season development program is an opportunity for teenage players (league age 13-16) to receive additional training and reps during the off-season. This hitting training will be led by legendary coach Ray Atkinson at Focus Baseball Facility (7806 Aurora Ave N, Seattle, Washington 98103).

NOTE: There will be two Winter Training for Teen Baseball programs in 2025:

  • Winter Training I will run from November 9th to December 18th.
  • Winter Training II will run from January 4th to February 22nd.
