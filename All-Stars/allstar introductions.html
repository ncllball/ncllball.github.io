Each year, North Central Little League (NCLL) sponsors several teams to compete in the Little League District 8 Tournament. The All-Stars teams represent our league and may have the opportunity to advance to the state tournament.

Let this space be both an introduction to those players and teams and a place to celebrate their accomplishments. This landing page will introduce the 2025 teams, organized by age group (10U, 11U, 12U, Intermediate, Juniors, and Seniors) and then by sport (Baseball followed by Softball).
