Each year, North Central Little League (NCLL) sponsors several teams to compete in the Little League District 8 Tournament. The All-Stars teams represent our league and may have the opportunity to advance to the state tournament.

Let this space be both an introduction to those players and teams and a place to celebrate their accomplishments. This landing page will introduce future teams while the pages to follow will commemorate past teams.

All players who wish to be considered for an NCLL All-Stars team must opt in by completing the official NCLL All-Stars Player Application which can be found in the '2025 Season' Menu, also found here: Link.
