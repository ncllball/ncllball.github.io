## EMAIL TEMPLATE — SINGLE PLAYER

**Subject:** [Action Required] Update Guardian Info for {{PLAYER_FIRST}}'s Summerball26 Registration

---

Hi {{G1_FIRST}},

I'm reaching out because a technical error on June 10 accidentally wiped the guardian contact information from {{PLAYER_FIRST}} {{PLAYER_LAST}}'s Summerball26 registration. The registration itself is fine — the spot is confirmed — but we need you to re-enter the contact information fields so coaches and team coordinators have accurate contact info when teams are announced.

**Please log in and update the registration by June 28.**

### How to re-enter the information

1. Log in to **ncllball.com** with the email address you use for NCLL registrations
2. Go to **My Account → My Players**
3. Find **{{PLAYER_FIRST}} {{PLAYER_LAST}}** and click on their Summerball26 registration
4. Click **Edit Registration** (or "Update Answers")
5. Fill in the guardian information fields:
   - Guardian 1 first and last name
   - Guardian 1 phone and email
   - Guardian 1 relationship to player
   {{G2_LINE}}
6. Continue through the form and click **Save** at the end

The information you need to enter is the same as what you provided during the original registration.

If you have any trouble logging in or finding the registration, reply to this email or contact us at admin@ncllball.com.

Thank you for taking care of this quickly — we're looking forward to a great summer!

— NCLL Registrar
admin@ncllball.com
