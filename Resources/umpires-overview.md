## How to Become a Little League Umpire

You can't have a game without rules. And you can't have rules without someone to enforce them. We need you to do that for these kids. It's the best seat in the house — and, along with coaching, the best way to help the youngsters have a great game.

We have veteran umpires who will train you to do as much or as little as you want. Here's how to get started:

1. **Register as a volunteer** — [NCLL Volunteer Registration link] ← update each season
   We'll communicate with you and initiate a background check. Little League requires all volunteers to complete a simple electronic background check — it's free and takes only a few minutes.

2. **Contact the Umpire Coordinator** — [umpcoordinator@ncllball.com]
   They'll subscribe you to our Google Group for training updates and communications.

We'll take it from there.
