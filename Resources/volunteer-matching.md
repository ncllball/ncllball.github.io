# Employer Volunteer Matching
<!-- Companion to volunteer-opportunities.md. List was current as of 2024–25 season — verify company programs annually. -->

Many companies match not only monetary donations to nonprofits, but also their employees' volunteer hours with cash grants. These programs — often called **Volunteer Match** or **Time Matching** — can significantly benefit NCLL.

In Washington, a volunteer hour is valued at **$27.54** (per Independent Sector). If a coach or team parent volunteers an average of 10 hours per week, that's over **$275 per week**. Over a four-month season, that adds up to **$4,400** — funds your employer could be donating to NCLL just for the time you're already giving.

## Does Your Employer Offer Volunteer Matching?

Every company has different processes for signing up or reporting hours. Even if your employer isn't listed below, check with your **Human Resources** department to see if they participate.
