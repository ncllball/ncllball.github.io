Meet Our 2026 Sponsors

North Central Little League runs on the generosity of local businesses who believe in giving kids a place to play, compete, and grow. This year we're proud to introduce the companies and neighbors who are making the 2026 season possible — as diverse as the neighborhoods we call home.

Phinney Ridge & Greenwood

The heart of NCLL country — and our deepest bench of supporters.

Bongos

A Caribbean playground tucked into a repurposed gas station on Aurora Ave N. The sandpit patio, jerk chicken, and rice bowls have made it a neighborhood staple since it opened.

56th Street Market

The best kind of neighborhood store since 2011 — open seven days a week at the corner of N 56th St, stocking staples plus a deep selection of gluten-free and specialty goods, plus beer and wine delivery.

Ben's Bread Co.

Started as a sourdough pop-up, now a storefront on N 70th St. Co-owner Ben Campbell is a James Beard semifinalist — handmade loaves, pastries, and Wednesday night pizza using natural leavening and locally grown grains.

Phinney Books

Opened in 2014 by eight-time Jeopardy! champion Tom Nissley at 7405 Greenwood Ave N. Carefully curated shelves cover fiction, kids' books, nature, and Northwest titles.

Focus Baseball

A Seattle-based nonprofit baseball academy at 7806 Aurora Ave N running high-quality training programs for all ages, with competitive select teams at the 13U–15U level through Perfect Game.

Kitch Switch

A design-build kitchen remodeling firm led by award-winning designer Sheley and 30-year construction veteran Jon. Stylish, fast kitchen transformations — free consultations at kitch-switch.com.

Greenwood Heating & Air

Keeping Seattle homes warm since 1918. More than a century later they still offer furnace service, heat pumps, mini-splits, and plumbing — (206) 784-1818.

Tori Roper Hair Studio

A solo chair studio at 7106 Greenwood Ave N, run by stylist Tori Roper who has been cutting and coloring hair since 2011. Personalized cuts and color with a lifestyle-first approach — a neighborhood staple in the heart of Greenwood.

Green Lake

Fuller Living Construction

Seattle's top-rated design-build remodeler on Google, operating from 5304 Green Lake Way N since 2010. Kitchen and bath renovations to full home additions — ranked in the top 4% of all licensed Washington contractors.

Green Lake Orthodontics

Dr. Sara Cassidy's boutique practice at 7900 E Green Lake Drive N. A VIP Diamond Plus Invisalign provider treating patients of all ages — braces, Invisalign, and lingual braces, with espresso in the waiting room.

Zoka Coffee

A Seattle specialty coffee roaster since 1997, when Jeff Babcock opened the original Green Lake café with a mission to serve truly fresh-roasted coffee. Small-batch roasting, direct-trade relationships with farmers, and four Seattle-area cafés.

Wallingford & Fremont

Murphy's Pub

Seattle's oldest Irish pub since 1981 at 1928 N 45th St. Family and dog-friendly with live weekly music, classic pub fare (the Reuben is legendary), and a staff that feels like family.

Cedarhouse Flowers

Lush, artful arrangements from their Wallingford shop at 1621 N 45th St. Colorful everyday arrangements and modern, feminine wedding flowers nestled between Green Lake, Fremont, and the U-District.

Lighthouse Roasters

Roasting fine coffee in a 120-year-old craftsman building at 400 N 43rd St since 1993. Notably wifi-free, it remains one of Seattle's great gathering spots — body, flavor, and acidity in perfect balance.

Javila Creer — Windermere

Managing Broker and consistent top-1% Seattle agent at Windermere Midtown, 1920 N 34th St. Licensed since 2003, specializing in the urban Seattle neighborhoods NCLL calls home.

Ballard

Reuben's Brews

Award-winning brewpub at 5010 14th Ave NW in the heart of the Ballard Brewery District. 28 rotating taps, scratch-made pub food, and a family and dog-friendly patio since 2012.

Harjo Construction

An award-winning residential design-build firm at 4900 15th Ave NW in Ballard, founded in 2010. Over 300 completed projects across North Seattle — kitchens, baths, additions, basement conversions, and ADUs.

North Seattle & Crown Hill

El Camion

Some of Seattle's most celebrated authentic Mexican food on Aurora Ave N. Tacos, burritos, gorditas, mulitas, tamales, shrimp ceviche, and a full catering operation — praised by The Seattle Times.

Childish Things

North Seattle's favorite children's resale boutique since 2008 at 9776 Holman Road NW. Women-owned, carrying new and gently-used clothing, gear, shoes, and toys for kids and expecting parents.

Queen Anne & Interbay

Crystal Panes

Seattle's go-to for residential and commercial window cleaning since 1985. Family-owned at 2212 Queen Anne Ave N — window cleaning, gutters, pressure washing, and five consecutive Angie's List Super Service Awards.

Puffin Pediatrics

Community-focused concierge pediatric clinic founded by Dr. Matthew Fradkin in 2024. Hybrid insurance and membership model at 1631 15th Ave W — small, accessible, open weekdays and Saturday mornings.

Wessco Blinds

Custom blinds, shades, shutters, and motorized treatments for over 30 years. Showroom at 210 Nickerson St with free in-home estimates across greater Seattle.

Across Seattle

Dick's Drive-In

A Seattle institution since January 28, 1954. Still family-owned, still Washington-only — hand-cut fries and made-to-order milkshakes at the same iconic stands for over 70 years.

Tutta Bella

The Northwest's first certified Neapolitan pizzeria since 2004. Pies cooked for 90 seconds in 800° wood-fired ovens — a go-to for families, date nights, and post-game celebrations across Seattle and the Eastside.

Merlino Foods

Family-owned foodservice purveyor with roots going back to 1900. From their 98,000-square-foot SODO warehouse they supply hundreds of Seattle restaurants with 22 varieties of mozzarella and specialty Italian imports.

Cutter & Buck

Founded on Seattle's working waterfront in 1989 — named for the sea (cutter) and the mountains (buck). Performance sportswear and golf apparel in professional team stores worldwide, still headquartered in Seattle.

CubeSmart

Self-storage across Seattle including 9309 Aurora Ave N, close to our fields. Climate-controlled units, drive-up access, and 24/7 video monitoring.

Compass Construction

Kirkland-based general contracting with 27 years across the Puget Sound. 135 team members, 80% repeat client rate, specializing in residential and mixed-use construction.

Foushée

One of the Puget Sound's leading commercial construction firms since 1977. Office buildouts, retail renovation, and LEED-certified projects — a reputation built on hard work, integrity, and on-time delivery.

CONTINUUM Fitness & Strength

Owner Zach Korte builds individualized workout and nutrition plans around "Strength in simplicity." A valued NCLL partner for years — helping launch AAA/Majors Winterball training sessions.

Orca Analytics

Seattle-based AI-powered growth tools for ecommerce brands. Consolidates data from Shopify, Meta, Google, and TikTok into one hub so brands can optimize spend and scale profitably.

Bear Mountain Capital

Seattle-based independent wealth management led by CFA Joe Day. Always a fiduciary — financial planning, portfolio management, and sustainable investing for individuals, small businesses, and institutions.

Seattle Police Department

Our officers walk the beat, respond to calls, and keep our community safe every single day. We're proud to have SPD's support on and off the field.

Project 9 Brewing Co.

Award-winning family- and dog-friendly brewery at 1409 NE 80th St. Great craft beer alongside Full Tank Coffee, with Mexican Seoul for food — a neighborhood gem in Maple Leaf/Roosevelt.

Lincoln High School Baseball

Lincoln's Lynx compete in the WIAA with a 9-3 record in 2024-25. Their partnership with NCLL reflects a shared commitment to developing Seattle's next generation of ballplayers.

JustCoachNow

Connects young athletes with elite college-athlete coaches for private training. Built on the idea that access to high-quality instruction shouldn't be limited to a select few — a mission that aligns with NCLL's.

Seattle Saver Magazine

Helping Seattle-area families find local deals, discounts, and community resources. We appreciate their support in spreading the word about North Central Little League.

Valley & Company Events

A Seattle special event planning and design studio founded in 2003 by husband-and-wife team Aleah and Nick Valley. Named among the Top Planners in the World by Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and Martha Stewart Weddings.

Thank you to every sponsor, returning and new, who believes that a season of baseball and softball is worth investing in. NCLL is a community effort — and this list proves it.
