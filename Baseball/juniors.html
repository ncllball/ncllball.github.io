Welcome to the Juniors Division of our little league baseball program! This division is specifically designed for players league age 13 and 14. As with all our divisions, we focus on player development while introducing more competitive elements of the game.

Juniors will follow the green book rules set forth by Little League International. The following is provided as clarification or exceptions.

Field Specifications

  • Games are played on a 90-foot diamond
  • Pitcher's mound is 60 feet, 6 inches from home plate
  • Games are typically played at either Greenlake or Lower Woodland Field #1

Competition Format

  • North Central teams compete against other teams from District 8
  • District 8 includes teams from:
    • Ballard
    • Queen Anne
    • Magnolia
    • Richmond
    • Northwest Seattle
    • Northeast Seattle

Team Formation

  • All players must participate in tryouts for placement purposes
  • Every player will be placed on a team
  • Teams are formed through a draft process
  • The goal is to create parity among the teams

Game Rules

Pitching & Battery Note: For daily pitch count limits, early‑season ramp guidance, and catcher ↔ pitcher (battery) movement restrictions, reference the consolidated Pitching Reference (see also battery eligibility section there).

Time Limits

  • Games are 7 innings
  • No new inning after 2 hours and 15 minutes

Minimum Play Requirements

  • Every player must play at least 6 defensive outs
  • Every player must have at least one at-bat

Equipment Requirements

  • BBCOR -3 bats only
  • Proper protective equipment required for all catchers
  • Metal cleats are permitted

Final Thoughts

The Juniors Division represents a significant step up in competition and skill development. Players will experience a more authentic version of baseball while maintaining the core values of Little League: character, courage, and loyalty.
